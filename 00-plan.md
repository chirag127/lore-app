# KnowledgeAtlas Master Plan

**Purpose:** A structured knowledge repository with book summaries, analysis, and narration for lifelong learners. Every book is classified into a strict two-level taxonomy of 11 top-level categories with numbered subcategories.

## Taxonomy

- 11 top-level categories (numbered 01-11)
- Each category has 4-14 subcategories
- Books live directly inside subcategories
- No third-level nesting

## The 11 Categories

| # | Category | Subcats | Focus |
|---|---|---|---|
| 01 | Learning & Productivity | 7 | Meta-learning, habits, deep work, creativity |
| 02 | Psychology & Human Behavior | 9 | Cognitive, social, clinical, developmental |
| 03 | Decision Making & Systems Thinking | 8 | Mental models, probability, risk, rationality |
| 04 | Philosophy & Religion | 7 | Western, Eastern, Indian, ethics, logic |
| 05 | Health, Medicine & Life Sciences | 9 | Medicine, nutrition, neuroscience, longevity |
| 06 | Finance & Investing | 13 | Personal finance, value investing, risk, markets |
| 07 | Business, Management & Entrepreneurship | 10 | Startups, strategy, leadership, marketing |
| 08 | Communication, Language & Writing | 8 | Writing craft, storytelling, persuasion, linguistics |
| 09 | History, Society & Politics | 10 | World history, sociology, political science, biography |
| 10 | Mathematics, Science & Technology | 14 | Math, physics, CS, software engineering, AI |
| 11 | Arts, Media, Biography & Fiction | 10 | Fiction, poetry, music, film, design, memoir |

## Numbering Rules

- Categories: 01, 02, ..., 11
- Subcategories: 01, 02, ... within each category
- Books: 001, 002, ... within each subcategory
- Files: 01-index.mdx, 02-content.mdx, 03-analysis.mdx, 04-narration.mdx, 05-meta.json

## Reading Order

The categories are ordered for progressive learning:
1. Start with 01 (how to learn)
2. Then 02-03 (how your mind works)
3. Then 04 (worldview foundations)
4. Then 05 (your body)
5. Then 06-07 (money and work)
6. Then 08 (expression)
7. Then 09 (context)
8. Then 10 (tools)
9. Then 11 (culture)
