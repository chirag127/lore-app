# Category & Subcategory Generation

## Category Index (01-index.mdx)
- Category name and number
- Purpose: why this category exists
- Scope: what belongs here
- Exclusions: what does NOT belong here
- Related categories
- Reading order of subcategories
- Most important books in category
- Counterpoint books included

## Subcategory Index (01-index.mdx)
- Subcategory name and number
- Purpose and scope
- What books belong / don't belong
- Recommended reading order
- Minimum 2 books
- At least 1 counterpoint/opposing book

## Reading Order (02-reading-order.mdx)
- Rationale for sequencing
- Prerequisites between subcategories
- Suggested paths (casual / interested / deep)

## Book List (03-book-list.mdx)
- Complete numbered list of all books
- Author, year, difficulty
- Counterpoint flag
- Status (exists / needs generation)

## Anti-Duplication
- No book in two subcategories
- Primary assignment only
- Secondary cross-references OK in meta
