Exercise is the single most impactful lever for healthspan and cognitive function. This subcategory covers the physiology of strength and hypertrophy, cardiovascular and aerobic conditioning, flexibility and mobility work, injury prevention, and sports-specific performance. The literature ranges from research syntheses to practical programming methodologies.
