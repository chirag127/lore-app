Aging is increasingly understood as a treatable biological process rather than an inevitable decline. This subcategory covers the hallmarks of aging (senescence, telomere attrition, epigenetic alteration), interventions that extend healthspan (caloric restriction, exercise mimetics, senolytics), and the emerging clinical science of biomarker tracking and preventive medicine.
