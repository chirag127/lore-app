The cognitive brain does not exist separately from the physical body. This subcategory covers brain health and neuroplasticity across the lifespan, the physiology of stress and hormonal balance (cortisol, thyroid, sex hormones), and the emerging literature on psychedelics and consciousness as tools for mental health.
