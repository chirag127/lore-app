Nutrition is the most debated domain in health because the science is young, the incentives are distorted, and individual variation is real. This subcategory covers macronutrient and micronutrient science, evidence-based dietary frameworks (Mediterranean, low-carb, plant-based), the gut microbiome and metabolic health, intermittent fasting and time-restricted eating, and the critical skill of evaluating nutritional research.
