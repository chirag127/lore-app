Sleep is the single most underrated performance and health intervention. This subcategory covers sleep biology (NREM/REM architecture, glymphatic clearance), circadian mechanisms and their genetic basis, common sleep disorders (insomnia, sleep apnea), and evidence-based sleep hygiene practices — the behavioral and environmental levers for better sleep.
