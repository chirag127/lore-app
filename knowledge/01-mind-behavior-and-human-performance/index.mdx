# 🧠 Mind, Behavior & Human Performance

## 📚 Overview

This category is the foundation of BookAtlas. It brings together three deeply
interconnected domains — **learning & productivity**, **psychology & human
behavior**, and **decision making & systems thinking** — into a single coherent
shelf. The central question: *How does the human mind work, and how can we
make it work better?*

Whether you want to learn faster, build better habits, think more clearly,
understand yourself and others, avoid cognitive traps, or see the world in
systems, these books provide the evidence, frameworks, and practices.

## 🔗 Category Boundaries

- **02 Body & Health** takes the **clinical/medical** side: psychiatry instead
  of clinical psychology, biological neuroscience instead of cognitive
  psychology, physical health instead of mental performance.
- **06 Philosophy** takes the **foundational/theoretical** side: philosophy of
  mind, epistemology, ethics — the "why" questions, not the "how" questions.
- **04 Computers** takes **computational** models of mind (AI, cognitive
  architectures) when the focus is on building systems, not understanding humans.

## 📖 Major Topics

- **Learning & Skill Acquisition**: How people learn. Spaced repetition,
  deliberate practice, the science of expertise, meta-learning, study
  techniques that actually work.
- **Habits, Productivity & Focus**: How to build systems that make good
  behavior automatic. Attention management, deep work, energy management,
  knowledge management, goal setting.
- **Creativity & Innovation**: How new ideas emerge. The creative process,
  divergent thinking, incubation, constraints, creative confidence.
- **Psychology**: The full spectrum — cognition (memory, attention, language),
  behavior (conditioning, reinforcement), social (conformity, persuasion,
  groups), motivation (intrinsic/extrinsic, drive), emotion, personality,
  development, and positive psychology.
- **Mental Models & Decision Making**: The frameworks that help us think
  better. First principles, inversion, Occam's razor, circle of competence,
  second-order thinking.
- **Probability, Forecasting & Risk**: How to reason under uncertainty.
  Bayesian thinking, calibration, prediction markets, superforecasting.
- **Cognitive Biases & Debiasing**: The catalog of systematic errors in
  human judgment and how to mitigate them.
- **Systems Thinking**: Feedback loops, leverage points, stocks and flows,
  emergence, causal loop diagrams, cybernetics.
- **Game Theory**: Strategic interaction, cooperation and competition, Nash
  equilibria, evolutionary game theory.

## 🧠 Reading Order

1. Start with **Atomic Habits** + **Thinking, Fast and Slow** for the
   foundational framework
2. Move to **Deep Work** + **The Power of Habit** for productivity
3. Then **Predictably Irrational** + **Superforecasting** for decision making
4. Then **Thinking in Systems** for systems thinking
5. Then **The Art of Learning** for mastery
6. Finally specialized books based on interest

## 🚀 Why This Category Matters

Understanding the mind is the meta-skill. Every other domain — health, money,
business, relationships, science — depends on how well we think, learn, and
decide. This category pays the highest return on investment because it
improves everything else.
