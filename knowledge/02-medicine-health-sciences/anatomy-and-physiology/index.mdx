# Anatomy & Physiology

## Overview

Anatomy is the study of the structure of the human body — its organs,
tissues, and systems. Physiology is the study of how those structures
function, from the molecular to the whole-organism level. Together they
form the essential foundation for understanding health, disease, and
medical intervention. This subcategory covers human anatomy and systems
physiology.
