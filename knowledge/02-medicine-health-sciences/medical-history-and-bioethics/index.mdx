# Medical History & Bioethics

## Overview

Medical history traces the evolution of medical knowledge, practice, and
institutions from ancient healing traditions to modern scientific medicine.
Bioethics examines the moral questions raised by advances in medicine and
biology — informed consent, end-of-life care, resource allocation, genetic
engineering, and the physician-patient relationship. This subcategory
covers both the history of medicine and bioethical principles and medical
decision-making.
