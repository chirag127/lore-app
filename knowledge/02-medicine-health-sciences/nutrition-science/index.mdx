# Nutrition Science

## Overview

Nutrition science examines the interactions between nutrients (macro and
micro), diet, and the human body. It bridges biochemistry, physiology,
and public health. This subcategory covers macronutrient and micronutrient
science (carbohydrates, proteins, fats, vitamins, minerals), evidence-based
nutrition and dietetics (dietary guidelines, clinical nutrition research),
and clinical nutrition and metabolic medicine (nutritional support in
disease states, metabolic syndrome).
