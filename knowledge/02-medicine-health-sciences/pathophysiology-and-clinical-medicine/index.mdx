# Pathophysiology & Clinical Medicine

## Overview

Pathophysiology describes the functional changes that accompany disease —
how normal physiology is disrupted. Clinical medicine applies this
knowledge to diagnose and treat patients. This subcategory covers the
major branches: internal medicine and clinical reasoning, infectious
disease and epidemiology, oncology and cancer biology, surgical principles
and procedures, and emergency medicine and critical care.
