# Pharmacology

## Overview

Pharmacology studies how chemical substances interact with biological
systems — what the body does to a drug (pharmacokinetics) and what a drug
does to the body (pharmacodynamics). This subcategory covers drug discovery
and development (from target identification to clinical trials), clinical
pharmacology and drug interactions (how drugs are used in practice,
adverse effects, drug-drug interactions), and the mechanisms of action of
major drug classes.
