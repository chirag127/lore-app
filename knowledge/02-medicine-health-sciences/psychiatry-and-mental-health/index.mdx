# Psychiatry & Mental Health

## Overview

Psychiatry is the medical specialty devoted to mental disorders. It bridges
the biological understanding of brain function with the psychological
experience of the patient. This subcategory covers clinical psychology and
psychopathology (the classification and understanding of mental disorders),
evidence-based psychotherapy approaches (CBT, psychoanalysis, DBT, ACT),
and neuropharmacology and psychopharmacology (how psychiatric medications
work and their clinical use).
