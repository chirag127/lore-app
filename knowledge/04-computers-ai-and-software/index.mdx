# 💻 Computers, AI & Software

## 📚 Overview

This category covers the entire stack of modern computing — from the
theoretical foundations of computer science to the practical craft of
software engineering, from artificial intelligence and machine learning
to system design, cybersecurity, and general engineering. Whether you
want to understand how computers work, build better software, or master
the AI revolution, these books provide the essential knowledge.

## 🔗 Category Boundaries

- **07 Math, Logic & Science** covers the **mathematical foundations** that
  computing builds on: formal logic, discrete math, probability, statistics.
  This category covers the **applied** side.
- **05 Business** covers the **business of technology**: tech startups,
  platform strategy, product management.
- **08 Society** covers the **history and societal impact** of technology.
- **01 Mind** covers **human cognition**; AI is distinct in that it models
  and builds *artificial* cognition.

## 📖 Major Topics

- **Computer Science Theory**: Computation, complexity, automata, information
  theory, programming languages, compilers, operating systems.
- **Software Engineering**: Code quality, design patterns, testing, refactoring,
  API design, version control, engineering culture, technical debt.
- **System Design & Architecture**: Scalability, microservices, load balancing,
  caching, CAP theorem, consensus algorithms, event-driven architecture.
- **Artificial Intelligence**: Machine learning, deep learning, NLP, computer
  vision, reinforcement learning, AI safety, alignment, LLMs, transformers.
- **Data, Algorithms & Databases**: Data structures, algorithmic thinking,
  relational and NoSQL databases, query optimization, data pipelines,
  distributed storage.
- **DevOps & Platform Engineering**: CI/CD, infrastructure as code,
  containerization, orchestration, monitoring, observability, incident
  response, SRE principles.
- **Networking & Distributed Systems**: TCP/IP, HTTP, DNS, load balancing,
  consensus protocols (Paxos, Raft), distributed transactions, consensus.
- **Cybersecurity**: Cryptography, network security, application security,
  threat modeling, penetration testing, security operations.
- **Engineering**: Mechanical, electrical, civil, biomedical, robotics,
  materials science, energy systems — the broader engineering disciplines
  that computing interacts with.

## 🧠 Reading Order

1. Start with **The Pragmatic Programmer** + **Computer Science Distilled**
2. Then **Clean Code** for craftsmanship
3. Then **Designing Data-Intensive Applications** for systems thinking
4. Then **AI: A Modern Approach** for AI foundations
5. Then specialized deep dives based on your field

## 🚀 Why This Category Matters

Software is eating the world. Computing is the defining technology of our
era — and AI is its next frontier. Understanding the stack, from transistors
to transformers, is essential for anyone building, investing in, or making
policy for the digital world.
