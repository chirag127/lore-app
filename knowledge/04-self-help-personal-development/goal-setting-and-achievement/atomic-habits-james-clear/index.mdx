Atomic Habits is the definitive modern guide to behavior change. James Clear distills decades of research in biology, psychology, and neuroscience into a four-step framework that makes good habits inevitable and bad habits impossible. The book's central insight is that your outcomes are a lagging measure of your habits — tiny, 1% improvements compound into extraordinary results over time if you have the right system.
