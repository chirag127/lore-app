# Goal Setting & Achievement

## Overview

Goal setting is one of the most empirically validated techniques in personal development. This subcategory covers goal-setting frameworks (SMART goals, OKRs, BHAGs), the science of motivation (intrinsic vs extrinsic motivation, self-determination theory, the progress principle), and systems thinking for personal goals (how to design environments and habits that make achievement more likely). It bridges the psychology of motivation with practical productivity techniques.
