# Mental Health & Emotional Wellbeing

## Overview

This subcategory translates clinical research into practical strategies for managing everyday mental health challenges. It covers anxiety and stress management (relaxation techniques, cognitive restructuring, exposure principles), managing depression through behavioral activation (increasing rewarding activities, breaking rumination cycles), and building resilience and post-traumatic growth (developing coping skills, finding meaning in adversity). While informed by clinical psychology, it focuses on self-directed strategies rather than therapeutic treatment.
