Emotional Intelligence 2.0 delivers a clear, actionable framework for raising your EQ — the skill that research shows matters more than IQ for career success, leadership effectiveness, and relationship quality. Unlike Daniel Goleman's foundational 1995 book, which explained *what* emotional intelligence is, Bradberry and Greaves focus entirely on *how* to improve it. The book pairs 66 concrete strategies with the Emotional Intelligence Appraisal, an online assessment taken by over 3 million people, creating a complete measurement-to-improvement system. It has sold over 2.5 million copies, been translated into 25 languages, and earned endorsements from the Dalai Lama, Stephen Covey, and Patrick Lencioni.
