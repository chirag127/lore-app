# Mindset & Belief Systems

## Overview

Mindset research, popularized by Carol Dweck, examines how people's implicit beliefs about their abilities affect motivation, resilience, and achievement. A growth mindset — the belief that abilities can be developed through effort — contrasts with a fixed mindset — the belief that abilities are static. This subcategory extends to related concepts: cognitive reframing (changing how we interpret events), self-limiting beliefs (the internal narratives that hold us back), and the broader belief systems that structure personal identity and behavior.
