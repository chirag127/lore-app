# Relationships & Social Skills

## Overview

Relationships are central to human flourishing, yet they require skills that are rarely taught explicitly. This subcategory covers interpersonal skills and emotional intelligence (reading others, managing conflict, empathy), building and maintaining deep friendships (vulnerability, reciprocity, intentionality), and romantic relationships and attachment (attachment theory, communication, partnership dynamics). It draws on social psychology, clinical research, and the accumulated wisdom of relationship counseling.
