# Spirituality & Life Meaning

## Overview

This subcategory addresses what might be called existential self-help: the search for purpose, meaning, and a sense of significance in life. It covers finding purpose and meaning (drawing on Viktor Frankl's logotherapy, existential psychology, and purpose research), death, impermanence, and living fully (mortality salience, terror management, living authentically in the face of finitude), and spirituality without religion (secular spirituality, awe, connection, and transcendence outside traditional frameworks).
