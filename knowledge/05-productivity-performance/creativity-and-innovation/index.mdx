Creativity as a systematic discipline rather than a mysterious gift. The literature spans the creative process (from incubation to execution), design thinking methodologies, the nature of originality and taste, and the psychology of overcoming creative blocks. Key figures include Austin Kleon, Steven Pressfield, and the d.school's design thinking tradition.
