Time management assumes energy is infinite. Energy management recognizes that cognitive output depends on circadian rhythms, sleep quality, strategic rest, nutrition, and exercise. This subcategory covers chronobiology, ultradian rhythms, recovery protocols, and the science of matching tasks to physiological states.
