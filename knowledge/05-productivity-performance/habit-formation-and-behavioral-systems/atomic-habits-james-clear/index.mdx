Atomic Habits is the definitive modern guide to behavior change. James Clear distills decades of research in biology, psychology, and neuroscience into a four-step framework that makes good habits inevitable and bad habits impossible. The book's central insight is that your outcomes are a lagging measure of your habits — tiny 1% improvements compound into extraordinary results over time if you have the right system. With over 25 million copies sold and translations in 60+ languages, Atomic Habits has become the best-selling self-help book of the 2010s and the most popular habit-formation guide ever published.
