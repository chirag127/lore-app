The Happiness Advantage challenges the fundamental assumption that success leads to happiness. Shawn Achor, who taught the most popular course at Harvard on positive psychology, presents research showing that the causal arrow points the other direction: happiness fuels success. The book translates decades of research in positive psychology into seven actionable principles that rewire the brain to scan for opportunity, build resilience, and leverage social support. Published in 2010, it became one of the foundational texts of the positive psychology in the workplace movement and established Achor as a leading voice in the field.
