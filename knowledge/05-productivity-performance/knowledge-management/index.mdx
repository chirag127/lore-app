Knowledge management in the productivity context is the practice of capturing, organizing, and retrieving ideas for creative and analytical work. This subcategory covers the Zettelkasten method (Ahrens), the rise of digital tools (Roam Research, Obsidian, Notion), note-taking as thinking, and the design of personal knowledge management systems that compound over time.
