The Zettelkasten (slip-box) method originated by Niklas Luhmann and popularized by Sönke Ahrens. Modern digital implementations in Roam Research, Obsidian, Logseq, and others enable bi-directional linking, graph visualization, and emergent idea structures. Covers atomic notes, linking principles, and the practice of building a second brain.
