Spaced repetition is the most empirically robust learning technique. Covers the forgetting curve (Ebbinghaus), the spacing effect, the design of good flashcards (Minimum Information Principle, cloze deletions), and the practical use of Anki or other SRS tools for long-term knowledge retention in any domain.
