Ultralearning is an aggressive, self-directed approach to learning championed by Scott Young — intense projects that compress years of learning into months. Covers the nine principles (metalearning, focus, directness, drill, retrieval, feedback, retention, intuition, experimentation) and case studies of accelerated learning.
