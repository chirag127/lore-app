The original productivity subcategory, running from David Allen's GTD workflow to modern time-blocking and calendar architecture. Covers the Eisenhower Matrix for prioritization, the concept of zero inbox, batching and time-blocking methodologies, and the deeper question of what deserves your attention in a finite life. Essentialism and Four Thousand Weeks provide the philosophical anchor.
