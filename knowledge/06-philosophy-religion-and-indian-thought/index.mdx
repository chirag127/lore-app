# 🕉 Philosophy, Religion & Indian Thought

## 📚 Overview

This category explores humanity's deepest questions: What is real? What matters? How should we live? From ancient Greek philosophy to contemporary Buddhist thought, from monotheistic traditions to the wisdom of India, these books examine the foundations of human understanding.

The focus is on **philosophical reasoning** and **wisdom traditions** — not religious practice (that's 10) or scientific methodology (that's 07).

## 🔗 Category Boundaries

- **10 Fiction & Literature** covers **religious texts** and **spiritual practice**. This category covers **philosophical inquiry** and **wisdom traditions**.
- **07 Math, Logic & Science** covers **scientific methodology** and **empirical inquiry**. This category covers **conceptual analysis** and **philosophical reasoning**.
- **01 Mind, Behavior & Human Performance** covers **cognitive science**. This category covers **philosophy of mind**.

## 📖 Major Topics

- **Western Philosophy**: Ancient (Plato, Aristotle), Medieval (Aquinas), Modern (Descartes, Kant), Contemporary (Nozick, Rawls).
- **Eastern Philosophy**: Taoism, Confucianism, Zen Buddhism, Hindu Vedanta, Japanese philosophy.
- **Indian Philosophy**: Advaita Vedanta, Buddhism, Jainism, Nyaya, Mimamsa, Vedanga.
- **Ethics & Moral Philosophy**: Utilitarianism, deontology, virtue ethics, contractualism, care ethics.
- **Philosophy of Mind**: Consciousness, qualia, intentionality, mind-body problem, cognitive science.
- **Political Philosophy**: Liberty, equality, justice, state of nature, rights, democracy.
- **Metaphysics**: Reality, existence, identity, causation, time, space.
- **Epistemology**: Knowledge, belief, justification, skepticism, probability.

## 🧠 Reading Order

1. Start with **Sophies World** for philosophical foundations
2. Then **Meditations** for practical wisdom
3. Then **Beyond Good** for Nietzsche's perspective
4. Then **The Republic** for Plato's politics
5. Then specialized areas based on interest

## 🚀 Why This Category Matters

Philosophy is the discipline of thinking clearly about the most important questions. In an age of information overload and ideological polarization, the ability to reason carefully, examine assumptions, and think systematically is more valuable than ever. These books train the mind to think at the highest level.
