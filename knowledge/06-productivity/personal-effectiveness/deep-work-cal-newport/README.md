## About

*Deep Work: Rules for Focused Success in a Distracted World* (2016) argues that the ability to focus without distraction on cognitively demanding tasks is becoming simultaneously rarer and more valuable in the knowledge economy. Georgetown CS professor Cal Newport provides the rationale and four rules for cultivating this super-skill.
