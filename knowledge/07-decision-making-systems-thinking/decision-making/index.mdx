Decision making is the bridge between analysis and action. This subcategory covers multi-criteria decision analysis, decision trees, the OODA loop (Boyd), the Cynefin framework, the WRAP process (Heath brothers), regret minimization (Jeff Bezos), and systems for making decisions that are both rational and practical. The focus is on decision process rather than decision outcome — a good decision can have a bad outcome, but a good process consistently produces good outcomes over time.
