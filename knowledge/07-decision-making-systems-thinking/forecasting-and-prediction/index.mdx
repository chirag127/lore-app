Forecasting is the ultimate test of judgment: how well can we predict what will happen next? This subcategory covers the research of Philip Tetlock and the Good Judgment Project, which demonstrated that a small minority of "superforecasters" consistently outperform experts and algorithms through probabilistic thinking, frequent updating, intellectual humility, and a growth mindset about their own accuracy. Also covers prediction markets, scenario planning, and the epistemology of foresight.
