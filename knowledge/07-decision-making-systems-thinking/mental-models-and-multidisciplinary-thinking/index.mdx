Mental models are simplified representations of how the world works. The multidisciplinary thinker collects a lattice of these models from physics (inertia, critical mass), biology (evolution, natural selection), mathematics (Pareto principle, regression to the mean), economics (opportunity cost, comparative advantage), and other fields. Each model is a lens; the more lenses you have, the better you see. This approach, championed by Charlie Munger and systematized by Shane Parrish and the Farnam Street community, aims to equip the mind with a full toolbox for understanding complex reality.

- Algorithms to Live By (Brian Christian & Tom Griffiths)
- Seeking Wisdom (Peter Bevelin)
