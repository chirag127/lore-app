*Decision Quality* represents the institutional wisdom of Strategic Decisions Group (SDG), the leading consulting firm in decision analysis. The authors — including Carl Spetzler, SDG's founder and former chairman — synthesize decades of consulting experience into a framework for embedding decision quality at the organizational level.

<BookHeader />

Unlike most decision books that focus on individual cognition or specific analytical tools, *Decision Quality* addresses the organizational challenge: how do you make good decisions when multiple stakeholders, competing priorities, and political dynamics are at play?
