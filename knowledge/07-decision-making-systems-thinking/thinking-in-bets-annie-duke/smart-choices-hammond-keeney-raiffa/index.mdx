*Smart Choices* is the definitive popular introduction to multi-attribute decision analysis — the structured methodology for evaluating options across multiple, often conflicting, criteria. Written by three of the world's leading experts in decision science, it distills decades of academic research into an accessible 244-page guide.

<BookHeader />

The ProACT framework (Problem, Objectives, Alternatives, Consequences, Tradeoffs, Uncertainty, Risk Tolerance, Linked Decisions) provides a complete vocabulary and process for any decision. The book's power lies not in novelty but in comprehensiveness: it names every element that a good decision process must address and provides tools for each.
