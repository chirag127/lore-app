description: "The application of learning principles to change socially significant behavior."
---

ABA uses reinforcement, punishment, shaping, and chaining to increase desired behaviors and reduce maladaptive ones. Widely validated for autism intervention, organizational behavior management, and habit modification. Based on the operant conditioning framework of B.F. Skinner.
