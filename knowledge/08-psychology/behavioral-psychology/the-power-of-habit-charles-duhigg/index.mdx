*The Power of Habit: Why We Do What We Do in Life and Business* (2012) by Charles Duhigg reveals the science behind how habits work — and more importantly, how they can be changed. Drawing on decades of neurological research and compelling case studies from business, sports, and social movements, Duhigg shows that every habit follows a three-step loop of cue, routine, and reward, and that by understanding this loop we gain the power to reshape our behaviors.
