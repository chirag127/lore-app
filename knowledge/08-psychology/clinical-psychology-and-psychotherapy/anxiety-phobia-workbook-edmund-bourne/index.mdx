## Book Summary

**The Anxiety and Phobia Workbook: Proven Methods to Overcome Worry, Phobias, Panic Attacks, and Obsessions** (6th edition, 2020, New Harbinger Publications, ~464 pp; 4th edition 2009, ISBN 9781572248913) is the most widely used self-help CBT workbook for anxiety disorders, authored by clinical psychologist **Edmund J. Bourne, PhD**.

Since its first edition in 1990 — the winner of a **Benjamin Franklin Book Award for Excellence in Psychology** — the book has sold **over 1.5 million copies** in more than a dozen languages. Therapists worldwide recommend it, and it is regularly assigned as a structured adjunct to formal therapy for:

- **Panic disorder** and **agoraphobia**
- **Generalised Anxiety Disorder (GAD)**
- **Obsessive-Compulsive Disorder (OCD)**
- **Post-Traumatic Stress Disorder (PTSD)**
- **Social anxiety disorder**
- **Specific phobias** (heights, flying, animals, blood, etc.)
- **Health anxiety** (hypochondriasis) and chronic worry
- **Adjustment disorder** and stress management

The 6th edition is fully updated for **DSM-5** compatibility, incorporates the latest neuroscientific research, and adds significant new material on **mindfulness-based strategies**, **nutritional and exercise interventions**, and **medication considerations** — all while retaining the hands-on, write-in workbook format that is the book's defining feature.
