CBT, DBT, ACT, psychodynamic therapy, EMDR, and other empirically supported treatments. This subcategory covers treatment protocols, mechanisms of change, therapeutic alliance research, and the ongoing effort to base clinical practice on scientific evidence rather than theoretical allegiance.
