description: "The assessment, diagnosis, treatment, and prevention of mental disorders."
---

Clinical psychology integrates scientific research with therapeutic practice. It covers diagnostic classification systems (DSM-5, ICD), evidence-based psychotherapies (CBT, DBT, ACT, psychodynamic), and the study of trauma, resilience, and post-traumatic growth. Distinguished from psychiatry by its non-pharmacological emphasis and from counseling by its commitment to empirical validation of treatment outcomes.
