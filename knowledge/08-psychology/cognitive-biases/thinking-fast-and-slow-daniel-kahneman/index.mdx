*Thinking, Fast and Slow* is Daniel Kahneman's masterwork — a comprehensive synthesis of decades of research on human judgment and decision-making. The book introduces two metaphorical characters that live inside our heads: System 1, the fast, intuitive autopilot, and System 2, the slow, deliberate decision-maker. Through dozens of elegant experiments and real-world examples, Kahneman demonstrates that our minds are far less rational than we imagine.
