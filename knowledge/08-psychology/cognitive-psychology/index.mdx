---
title: "Cognitive Psychology"
parent: "08-psychology"
order: 3
description: "The study of mental processes including attention, memory, perception, language, and executive function."
---

Cognitive psychology investigates the internal mental processes that mediate between stimulus and response. Through experimental methods and computational modeling, it has produced detailed theories of how we perceive patterns, encode and retrieve memories, allocate attention, process language, and make decisions. The cognitive revolution transformed psychology from behaviorism into the modern science of mind.
