---
title: "Evolutionary Psychology"
parent: "08-psychology"
order: 5
description: "The study of how evolutionary pressures have shaped the human mind and behavior."
---

Evolutionary psychology applies Darwinian principles to understand the universal features of human psychology. It posits that the brain consists of specialized mental modules shaped by ancestral environments. Subtopics include mating strategies, parental investment theory, cooperation and altruism, intergroup conflict, and cross-cultural universals of emotion and cognition.
