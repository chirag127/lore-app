title: "Industrial-Organizational Psychology"
parent: "08-psychology"

description: "The application of psychological science to workplaces and organizations."
---

I-O psychology applies research on motivation, leadership, team dynamics, and decision-making to improve organizational effectiveness and employee well-being. It covers personnel selection and assessment, training evaluation, job design, organizational culture, leadership development, and the psychology of workplace motivation and engagement.
