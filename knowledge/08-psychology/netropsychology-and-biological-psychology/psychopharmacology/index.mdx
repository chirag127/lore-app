---
title: "Psychopharmacology"
parent: "08-psychology/neuropsychology-and-biological-psychology"
order: 3
description: "How drugs affect the brain and behavior."
---

The study of how psychoactive substances — therapeutic and recreational — alter neural function and psychological experience. Covers mechanisms of action of antidepressants, anxiolytics, antipsychotics, stimulants, and psychedelics, as well as the neurobiology of addiction and substance use disorders.
