description: "The five-factor taxonomy of personality traits: Openness, Conscientiousness, Extraversion, Agreeableness, Neuroticism."
---

The Big Five (OCEAN) is the most empirically validated model of personality structure. Covers the lexical hypothesis, factor-analytic evidence, heritability estimates, stability across the lifespan, and predictive validity for life outcomes including health, career success, and relationships.
