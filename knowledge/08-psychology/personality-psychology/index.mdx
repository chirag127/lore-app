---
title: "Personality Psychology"
parent: "08-psychology"
order: 8
description: "The study of individual differences in patterns of thinking, feeling, and behaving."
---

Personality psychology seeks to describe and explain the stable patterns that distinguish individuals. The Big Five model (OCEAN) provides the dominant taxonomic framework. Topics include temperament, character, personality development across the lifespan, personality disorders and their clinical typologies, and cross-cultural variation in personality expression.
