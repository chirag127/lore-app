description: "The classification and measurement of positive human character traits."
---

The VIA Classification of Character Strengths identifies 24 universally valued strengths organized under six core virtues: wisdom, courage, humanity, justice, temperance, and transcendence. Covers strength assessment, the relationship between strengths and well-being, and interventions for cultivating signature strengths.
