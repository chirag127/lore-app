description: "The psychological states and conditions of deep engagement and meaningful living."
---

Flow (Csikszentmihalyi) is the state of complete absorption in challenging, skill-matched activity. Eudaimonic well-being emphasizes meaning, personal growth, and self-realization over hedonic pleasure. Covers the conditions for flow, meaning-making processes, and the distinction between hedonic and eudaimonic happiness.
