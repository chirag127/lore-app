---
title: "Positive Psychology"
parent: "08-psychology"
order: 9
description: "The scientific study of what makes life worth living — well-being, character strengths, and human flourishing."
---

Launched by Martin Seligman, positive psychology shifts focus from pathology to human flourishing. It studies happiness, flow, meaning, character strengths (VIA Classification), gratitude, resilience, and the PERMA model of well-being. It provides an evidence-based counterweight to clinical psychology's traditional focus on disorder, offering frameworks for building the best things in life rather than repairing the worst.
