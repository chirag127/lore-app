The classic studies of social influence: Asch's line judgment experiments (conformity), Milgram's obedience studies, Zimbardo's Stanford Prison Experiment. Covers social identity theory, informational and normative social influence, minority influence, and the situational factors that predict when individuals will conform or obey.
