# Aesthetics

## Overview

Aesthetics examines the nature of art, beauty, and taste. It asks: what makes something art? What is aesthetic experience? Can judgments of taste be objective? It covers theories of art (formalism, expressionism, institutional theory), the ontology of artworks, and aesthetic experience in nature and everyday life. Aesthetics bridges philosophy with art history, musicology, and literary theory.
