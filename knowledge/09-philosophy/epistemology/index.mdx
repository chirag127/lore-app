# Epistemology

## Overview

Epistemology examines the nature, sources, and limits of knowledge. It asks: what does it mean to know something? What is the difference between knowledge, belief, and opinion? What counts as justification? Major traditions include rationalism (knowledge through reason), empiricism (knowledge through experience), and Kant's synthesis. Contemporary epistemology extends these questions to scientific knowledge, social knowledge, and the epistemology of disagreement.

## Books

1. **[The Structure of Scientific Revolutions](the-structure-of-scientific-revolutions-thomas-kuhn)** — Thomas S.
