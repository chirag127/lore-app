# Ethics & Moral Philosophy

## Overview

Ethics examines questions of right and wrong, good and evil, virtue and vice. Normative ethics proposes theories of right action (consequentialism, deontology, virtue ethics). Metaethics investigates the nature of moral language, truth, and psychology. Applied ethics brings philosophical tools to practical problems — bioethics, business ethics, AI ethics. This subcategory also covers the history of moral philosophy from Aristotle to contemporary debates.
