## Key Facts

- **Composed:** c. 350 BC
- **Structure:** 8 books; Books IV-VI = NE V-VII (common books); Books I-III, VII-VIII are unique
- **Signature concept:** *Kalokagathia* (noble goodness) — virtue-unity concept absent from the NE
- **Other unique content:** *Nemesis* as a virtue; analysis of *eutychia* (good fortune); tripartite classification of goods
- **Best English translation:** Anthony Kenny (Oxford World's Classics, 2011) — first complete translation
- **Named scholars referenced:** Anthony Kenny, Christopher Rowe, Brad Inwood, Raphael Woolf, Michael Woods, Harris Rackham, Filip Grgic, Hilde Vinje, Martha Nussbaum, John M. Cooper
