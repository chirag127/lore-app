# Metaphysics & Ontology

## Overview

Metaphysics investigates the most general features of reality: what kinds of things exist (ontology), the nature of time and space, causality and determinism, possibility and necessity, and the relationship between mind and world. Ontology, as a branch of metaphysics, catalogs the categories of being — objects, properties, events, universals, particulars. Metaphysical questions are among the oldest in philosophy and remain central to contemporary analytic and continental thought.
