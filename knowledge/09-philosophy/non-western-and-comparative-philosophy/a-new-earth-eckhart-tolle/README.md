## About the Book

*A New Earth: Awakening to Your Life's Purpose* is Eckhart Tolle's follow-up to *The Power of Now*. Where the first book focused on individual presence practice, this one expands to diagnose humanity's collective dysfunction — war, environmental destruction, interpersonal conflict — as stemming from identification with the ego. The book's central teaching: the ego is a false self constructed from thoughts and roles; awareness of this false self dissolves its grip; and individual awakening is the foundation of collective transformation.
