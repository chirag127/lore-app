# Philosophy of Mind & Language

## Overview

Philosophy of mind examines the nature of consciousness, mental states, and their relationship to the physical world — the mind-body problem, intentionality, qualia, and artificial intelligence. Philosophy of language investigates meaning, reference, truth, and the relationship between language, thought, and reality. These subfields are deeply interconnected: how we think about mind informs theories of meaning, and vice versa.
