## Key Concepts

- The Ladder of Love (Scala Amoris)
- Diotima's daimonic Eros
- The Form of Beauty
- Aristophanes' myth of the androgyne
- Alcibiades' encomium of Socrates
- Heavenly vs. Common Love (Pausanias)
- Physical and spiritual procreation
- The desire for immortality

## Recommended Reading Order

1. Read the dialogue itself first (any translation)
2. Then Vlastos (1973) "The Individual as Object of Love"
3. Then Nussbaum (1986) *The Fragility of Goodness*, ch. 6
4. Then Price (1989) *Love and Friendship in Plato and Aristotle*
5. Then Sheffield (2006) *Plato's Symposium: The Ethics of Desire*
