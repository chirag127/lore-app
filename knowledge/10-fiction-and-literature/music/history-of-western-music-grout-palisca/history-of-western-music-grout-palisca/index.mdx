*A History of Western Music* is the most widely used textbook for music history survey courses in North America. First published in 1960 by Donald Jay Grout, it has been continuously updated by Claude V. Palisca and J. Peter Burkholder through ten editions. The book covers the full sweep of Western classical music from ancient Greece to the twenty-first century, providing a comprehensive survey of composers, works, genres, and stylistic periods.
