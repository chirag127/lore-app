# Abrahamic Religions

## Overview

The Abrahamic religions share a common origin in the figure of Abraham and a commitment to monotheism, but they differ in their scriptures, doctrines, and historical development. Judaism, the oldest, is rooted in covenant and law. Christianity centers on Jesus Christ as messiah and savior. Islam, the youngest, proclaims Muhammad as the final prophet. Together they claim the allegiance of over half the world's population.
