# Atheism, Agnosticism & Secular Humanism

## Overview

This subcategory examines the philosophical and cultural positions that do not affirm theistic belief. Atheism denies the existence of God or gods. Agnosticism holds that the existence of God is unknown or unknowable. Secular humanism affirms human dignity, reason, and ethics without supernatural foundations. The subcategory covers the arguments for and against theism, the new atheist movement, and the ethical and political implications of secular worldviews.
