# Comparative Religion

## Overview

Comparative religion studies the world's religious traditions side by side, examining similarities and differences in doctrine, practice, and institutional development. It includes the history of religions (tracing the development of traditions over time), interfaith dialogue (seeking mutual understanding across traditions), and the sociology of religion (examining religion's role in contemporary society).
