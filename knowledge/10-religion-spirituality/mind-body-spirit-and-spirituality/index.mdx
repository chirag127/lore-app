# Mind, Body, Spirit & Spirituality

## Overview

This subcategory covers the broad terrain of contemporary spirituality that exists outside or alongside organized religion. It includes the mindfulness and meditation movement (drawing on Buddhist and Yogic traditions but adapted for secular contexts), and New Age and alternative spirituality (holistic healing, channeling, astrology, energy work). These practices emphasize personal experience and transformation rather than doctrine or institutional authority.
