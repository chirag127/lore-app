# Mysticism & Esoteric Traditions

## Overview

Mysticism concerns the direct, experiential knowledge of God, the Absolute, or ultimate reality — often through contemplative practice, asceticism, or initiatory transmission. Each major religious tradition has developed its own mystical stream: Christian mysticism (Meister Eckhart, Teresa of Avila), Islamic Sufism (Rumi, Ibn Arabi), Jewish Kabbalah, and Hindu mysticism and Tantra. These traditions often share structural similarities despite their doctrinal differences.
