# Commodities & Natural Resources

## Overview

Commodities investing covers physical assets like crude oil, natural gas,
precious and industrial metals, and agricultural products. This subcategory
examines the commodity futures markets, commodity equities, and the
macroeconomic drivers (supply, demand, geopolitics, inflation) that
influence commodity prices.
