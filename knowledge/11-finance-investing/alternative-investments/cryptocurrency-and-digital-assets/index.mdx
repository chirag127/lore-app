# Cryptocurrency & Digital Assets

## Overview

Cryptocurrencies and digital assets represent a new paradigm in finance
based on blockchain technology and decentralized consensus. This subcategory
covers Bitcoin and the theory of digital scarcity, Ethereum and smart
contracts, DeFi (decentralized finance), stablecoins, NFTs, and the
regulatory and investment considerations of this emerging asset class.
