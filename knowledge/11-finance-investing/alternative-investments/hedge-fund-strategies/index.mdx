# Hedge Fund Strategies

## Overview

Hedge funds employ a wide range of strategies beyond traditional long-only
investing. These include long/short equity, event-driven (merger arbitrage,
distressed securities), global macro (betting on macroeconomic trends),
relative value arbitrage, and managed futures. Each strategy has distinct
risk-return characteristics and requires specialized knowledge.
