# Alternative Investments

## Overview

Alternative investments encompass asset classes and strategies that fall
outside conventional stocks, bonds, and cash. They often offer
diversification benefits and return sources uncorrelated with traditional
markets. This subcategory covers hedge fund strategies (long/short, event-
driven, global macro, arbitrage), commodities and natural resources, and
cryptocurrency and digital assets as an emerging asset class.
