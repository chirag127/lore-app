# Derivatives & Structured Products

## Overview

Derivatives are contracts whose value is derived from the performance of an
underlying asset, index, or rate. They are essential tools for hedging,
speculation, and risk transfer. This subcategory covers options theory and
pricing models (Black-Scholes, binomial trees, Greeks), futures, forwards,
and swaps (interest rate swaps, currency swaps, commodities), and
volatility trading and the volatility risk premium.
