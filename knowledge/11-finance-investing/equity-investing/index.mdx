# Equity Investing

## Overview

Equity investing is the most accessible and widely practiced form of
investment. This subcategory covers the major schools of stock investing:
value investing (Graham, Buffett, Klarman philosophy and practice),
fundamental analysis (financial statement analysis, valuation, moats),
growth investing and quality investing, and quantitative equity strategies
(factor investing, systematic approaches).
