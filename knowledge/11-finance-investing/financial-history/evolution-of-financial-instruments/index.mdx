# Evolution of Financial Instruments

## Overview

Financial instruments have evolved over centuries, from simple loans and
bills of exchange to complex securitized products and digital assets. This
subcategory traces the history of stocks, bonds, derivatives, and structured
products, examining how each innovation addressed specific economic needs
and how financial engineering has shaped modern markets.
