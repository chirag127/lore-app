# History of Banking & Central Banking

## Overview

Banking has evolved from goldsmith safekeeping to the complex system of
commercial banking, investment banking, and central banking that underpins
the modern economy. This subcategory covers the origins of banking in
medieval Italy, the rise of central banks (Bank of England, Federal Reserve),
banking crises and regulation, and the evolution of monetary policy.
