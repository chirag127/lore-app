# Financial History

## Overview

Financial history provides the context for understanding today's markets
and the recurring patterns of boom, bust, and crisis. This subcategory
covers the history of banking and central banking (from the Medici to the
Federal Reserve), the evolution of financial instruments (stocks, bonds,
derivatives, and their markets), and the anatomy of market crashes, panics,
and financial crises from tulip mania to the 2008 global financial crisis.
