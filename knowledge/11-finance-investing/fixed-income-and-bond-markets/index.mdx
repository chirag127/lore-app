# Fixed Income & Bond Markets

## Overview

Fixed income is the largest securities market in the world. It encompasses
government bonds (Treasuries, sovereign debt), corporate bonds, and the
analytical tools for pricing and risk management. This subcategory covers
government bonds and sovereign debt markets, corporate bonds and credit
analysis (credit ratings, spreads, default risk), and yield curve dynamics,
duration, and interest rate risk management.
