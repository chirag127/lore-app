# Indian Finance

## Overview

India's financial markets have unique characteristics shaped by its
regulatory framework, economic history, and demographic dynamics. This
subcategory covers the structure and regulation of Indian equity markets,
including the role of SEBI, stock exchanges (BSE, NSE), and the unique
aspects of investing in India from taxation to mutual funds to
international investing considerations.
