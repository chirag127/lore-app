# Investment Theory & Principles

## Overview

Investment theory provides the intellectual framework for making informed
capital allocation decisions. This subcategory covers modern portfolio
theory (Markowitz optimization, efficient frontier), the efficient market
hypothesis and its critiques (including the behavioral and value investing
perspectives), and principles of long-term investing including dollar-cost
averaging. These ideas form the bedrock of both academic finance and
practical investment management.
