# Currency Markets & FX Strategy

## Overview

The foreign exchange (FX) market is the largest and most liquid financial
market in the world. This subcategory covers the structure of the FX market,
the drivers of exchange rates (interest rate differentials, trade balances,
capital flows, sentiment), FX carry trade and other strategies, and the
role of central banks in currency markets.
