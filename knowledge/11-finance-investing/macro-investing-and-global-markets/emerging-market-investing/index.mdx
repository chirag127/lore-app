# Emerging Market Investing

## Overview

Emerging markets account for a growing share of global GDP and offer
diversification and growth potential, but come with unique risks: political
instability, currency volatility, weaker legal protections, and less mature
capital markets. This subcategory covers the analysis of emerging market
equities, debt, and currencies, and the frameworks for managing the
additional risks.
