# Macro Investing & Global Markets

## Overview

Macro investing bases investment decisions on top-down analysis of economic
trends, monetary policy, geopolitics, and global capital flows. This
subcategory covers macro trading and global asset allocation (the art of
positioning portfolios for macroeconomic scenarios), currency markets and
foreign exchange strategy, and emerging market investing — the
opportunities and risks of investing in developing economies.
