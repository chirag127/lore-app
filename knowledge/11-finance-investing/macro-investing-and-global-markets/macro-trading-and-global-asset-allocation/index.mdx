# Macro Trading & Global Asset Allocation

## Overview

Macro trading involves taking positions based on views about interest rates,
growth, inflation, and geopolitical developments. Global asset allocation
determines how to distribute a portfolio across stocks, bonds, commodities,
currencies, and other asset classes based on macroeconomic conditions. This
subcategory covers discretionary macro, systematic macro, and risk parity
approaches.
