# Personal Finance & Wealth Building

## Overview

Personal finance is the application of financial principles to an
individual's or family's economic life. This subcategory covers budgeting,
saving, and building emergency funds, debt elimination and credit
management, the financial independence and early retirement (FIRE)
movement, and the classic personal finance literature that has shaped how
millions think about money and wealth.
