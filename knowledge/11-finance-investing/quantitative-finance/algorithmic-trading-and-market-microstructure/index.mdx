# Algorithmic Trading & Market Microstructure

## Overview

Algorithmic trading uses computer programs to execute trades based on
predefined rules. Market microstructure studies the process by which prices
are formed in financial markets — order books, bid-ask spreads, liquidity,
and the behavior of different market participants. This subcategory covers
execution algorithms, high-frequency trading, and the economics of
trading.
