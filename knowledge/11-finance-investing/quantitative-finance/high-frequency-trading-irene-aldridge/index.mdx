## Overview

*High-Frequency Trading* by Irene Aldridge is a practitioner's guide
to the world of HFT. The book covers the technology, strategies,
and infrastructure required for trading at microsecond time scales.
Aldridge explains how HFT firms operate, what strategies they use,
and how they manage the unique risks of ultra-fast trading.

The book bridges the gap between academic microstructure theory and
the practical realities of building HFT systems. It covers hardware
(FGPAs, network adapters), software (algorithm design, order
management), and strategy (market making, arbitrage, event trading).

## Executive Summary

Aldridge's central argument is that HFT is fundamentally a
technology business. The firms that succeed invest more in hardware,
co-location, and optimized code than in sophisticated financial
models. The book provides a complete framework for understanding
and building HFT systems, from strategy idea through infrastructure
deployment.
