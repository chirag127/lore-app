# Quantitative Finance

## Overview

Quantitative finance applies advanced mathematics, statistics, and
computational methods to financial problems. This subcategory covers
mathematical finance and stochastic calculus (the continuous-time
framework for pricing derivatives), algorithmic trading and market
microstructure (execution strategies, order book dynamics, latency),
and quantitative risk management including Value-at-Risk, Expected
Shortfall, and tail risk modeling.
