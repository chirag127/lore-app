# Mathematical Finance & Stochastic Calculus

## Overview

Mathematical finance provides the rigorous mathematical framework for
pricing financial instruments and managing risk. It draws heavily on
stochastic calculus (Ito's lemma, stochastic differential equations),
martingale theory, and partial differential equations. The Black-Scholes-
Merton framework for option pricing is the most famous application.
