# Real Estate Investing

## Overview

Real estate is one of the oldest and largest asset classes. This subcategory
covers the full spectrum: residential and commercial real estate (direct
ownership, leasing, property management), real estate investment trusts
(REITs) as a liquid way to access real estate returns, and real estate
private equity (value-add, opportunistic, and development strategies).
