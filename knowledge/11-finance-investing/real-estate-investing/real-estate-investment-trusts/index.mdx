# Real Estate Investment Trusts

## Overview

Real Estate Investment Trusts (REITs) offer a liquid way to invest in real
estate through publicly traded securities. REITs own and operate
income-producing properties and are required to distribute most of their
income as dividends. This subcategory covers REIT valuation, the different
REIT sectors (equity, mortgage, hybrid), and REIT investing strategies.
