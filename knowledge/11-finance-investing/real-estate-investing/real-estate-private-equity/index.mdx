# Real Estate Private Equity

## Overview

Real estate private equity involves investing in properties through private
funds rather than public markets. Strategies range from core (stable,
income-producing assets) to core-plus, value-add (renovation and
repositioning), opportunistic (development, distressed), and debt
strategies. This subcategory covers fund structures, underwriting, asset
management, and exit strategies.
