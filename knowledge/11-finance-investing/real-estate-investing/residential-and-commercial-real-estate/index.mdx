# Residential & Commercial Real Estate

## Overview

Direct real estate investment involves purchasing physical properties for
income and appreciation. Residential includes single-family homes,
multi-family, and apartments. Commercial includes office, retail, industrial,
and hospitality properties. This subcategory covers property valuation,
financing, due diligence, property management, leasing, and the legal and
tax aspects of real estate ownership.
