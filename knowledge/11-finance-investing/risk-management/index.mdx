# Risk Management

## Overview

Risk management is the discipline of understanding and navigating
uncertainty. It spans quantitative methods (VaR, stress testing) and
philosophical perspectives on probability, uncertainty, and the limits of
prediction. This subcategory covers the works of Nassim Taleb on
antifragility, black swans, and skin in the game, which have reshaped how
we think about risk, uncertainty, and decision-making in finance and
beyond.
