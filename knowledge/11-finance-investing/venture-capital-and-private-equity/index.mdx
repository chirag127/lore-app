# Venture Capital & Private Equity

## Overview

Venture capital and private equity provide capital to companies that are
not publicly traded, and work to create value through strategic, financial,
and operational improvements. Venture capital focuses on early-stage,
high-growth startups. Private equity encompasses leveraged buyouts, growth
equity, and distressed investing. This subcategory covers startup valuation
and deal structuring.
