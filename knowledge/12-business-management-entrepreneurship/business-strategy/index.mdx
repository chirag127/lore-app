# Business Strategy

## Overview

Business strategy examines how organizations position themselves in markets
to achieve lasting competitive advantage. It answers the fundamental
questions: where to compete and how to win. This subcategory covers the
major strategic frameworks: competitive strategy (Porter's five forces,
generic strategies, value chain), blue ocean strategy (creating
uncontested market space), and strategic positioning and competitive
dynamics.
