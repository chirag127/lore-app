# Case Interview Preparation

## Overview

The case interview is the defining feature of management consulting
recruiting. Candidates are given a business problem and evaluated on their
structure, analytical thinking, creativity, and communication. This
subcategory covers the common case types (profitability, market entry,
pricing, growth), frameworks for structuring analysis, quantitative skills,
and practice strategies for case interviews.
