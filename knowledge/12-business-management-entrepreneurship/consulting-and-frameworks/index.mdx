# Consulting & Frameworks

## Overview

Management consulting applies structured problem-solving to business
challenges. Consultants bring analytical rigor, industry knowledge, and
tested frameworks to diagnose problems and recommend solutions. This
subcategory covers management consulting methodologies (MECE, issue trees,
hypothesis-driven consulting), case interview preparation (the structured
approach to business case problems), and the McKinsey-style problem-solving
approach that has defined modern strategy consulting.
