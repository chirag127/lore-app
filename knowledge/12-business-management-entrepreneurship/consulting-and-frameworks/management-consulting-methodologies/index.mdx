# Management Consulting Methodologies

## Overview

Management consultants bring structured problem-solving to organizations.
This subcategory covers the core methodologies: MECE (mutually exclusive,
collectively exhaustive) structuring, issue trees and hypothesis-driven
consulting, profitability frameworks, growth analytics, and the strategic
frameworks (BCG matrix, Porter's five forces, SWOT, GE-McKinsey matrix)
that consultants use to analyze business problems.
