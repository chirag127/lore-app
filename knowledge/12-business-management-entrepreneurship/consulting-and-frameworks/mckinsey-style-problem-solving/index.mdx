# McKinsey-Style Problem Solving

## Overview

McKinsey & Company has developed a distinctive problem-solving approach
based on hypothesis-driven analysis. This subcategory covers the McKinsey
problem-solving process: defining the problem, structuring it with issue
trees, developing hypotheses, designing analyses to test hypotheses,
conducting interviews and research, synthesizing findings, and
communicating recommendations with storylines and pyramid-structured
presentations.
