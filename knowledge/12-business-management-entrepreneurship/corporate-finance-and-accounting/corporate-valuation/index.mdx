# Corporate Valuation

## Overview

Valuation is the process of determining the economic value of a business.
This subcategory covers the core valuation methodologies: discounted cash
flow (DCF) analysis, comparable company analysis (trading comps), precedent
transaction analysis (deal comps), leveraged buyout (LBO) analysis, and
sum-of-the-parts valuation. It also covers the conceptual frameworks for
understanding what drives value and how to model it.
