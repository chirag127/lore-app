# Financial Statements

## Overview

Financial statements are the primary means by which companies communicate
their financial performance to stakeholders. This subcategory covers the
three core statements: the balance sheet (assets, liabilities, equity),
the income statement (revenues, expenses, profits), and the cash flow
statement (operating, investing, financing cash flows). It also covers
ratio analysis, accrual accounting principles, and common adjustments.
