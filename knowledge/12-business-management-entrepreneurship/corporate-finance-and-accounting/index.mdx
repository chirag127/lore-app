# Corporate Finance & Accounting

## Overview

Corporate finance concerns the financial decisions of corporations: how to
raise capital, where to invest it, and how to return value to shareholders.
Accounting provides the language for measuring and communicating financial
performance. This subcategory covers financial statement analysis (reading
and interpreting balance sheets, income statements, cash flow), corporate
valuation (DCF, comparables, LBO modeling), and mergers and acquisitions
(strategy, process, integration).
