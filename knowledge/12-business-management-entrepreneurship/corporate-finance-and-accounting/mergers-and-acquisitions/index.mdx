# Mergers & Acquisitions

## Overview

Mergers and acquisitions (M&A) are corporate transactions in which companies
combine or one company acquires another. This subcategory covers M&A
strategy (why companies acquire), the deal process (target identification,
due diligence, valuation, negotiation, structuring), financing (cash, stock,
debt), integration, and the regulatory and legal aspects of M&A.
