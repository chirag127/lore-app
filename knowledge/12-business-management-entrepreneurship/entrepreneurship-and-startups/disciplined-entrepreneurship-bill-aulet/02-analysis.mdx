## Strengths

- **Actionable framework.** Each of the 24 steps is a concrete
  activity, not an abstract concept.
- **Systematic approach.** Provides structure where many founders
  rely on intuition.
- **Proven methodology.** Based on MIT's entrepreneurship program
  and validated by thousands of startups.
- **Comprehensive.** Covers everything from market selection to
  scaling.

---

## Weaknesses

- **Can feel overly prescriptive.** Entrepreneurship is messier than
  24 sequential steps suggest.
- **Light on the human element.** Company culture, psychological
  challenges of founding, and team dynamics are not addressed.
- **No code or technical depth.** Assumes a business focus rather
  than technical execution.
- **Linear presentation.** Real startups loop back constantly; the
  book's structure can be misleading.

---
