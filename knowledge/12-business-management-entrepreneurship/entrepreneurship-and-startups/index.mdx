# Entrepreneurship & Startups

## Overview

Entrepreneurship is the pursuit of opportunity without regard to resources
currently controlled. This subcategory covers the theory and practice of
building new ventures: lean startup methodology and customer development,
bootstrapping and capital-efficient startups, startup fundraising and
investor relations, founder psychology and resilience, indie hacking and
solo founders, and the classic works on entrepreneurship.
