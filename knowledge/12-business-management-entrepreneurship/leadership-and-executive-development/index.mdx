# Leadership & Executive Development

## Overview

Leadership is the capacity to translate vision into reality. This subcategory
covers the major theories and practices of leadership: executive leadership
and corporate governance, building trust and psychological safety, leading
teams through change, emotional intelligence, and the literature on what
makes great leaders — from military leadership to servant leadership to
radical candor.
