# Management & Organizational Behavior

## Overview

Management is the practice of getting things done through and with people.
Organizational behavior studies how individuals and groups act within
organizations. This subcategory covers the essential management disciplines:
performance management and goal-setting (OKRs, KPIs), motivation theory
(intrinsic vs. extrinsic motivation, self-determination theory),
organizational design and structure, and the classic works of Peter Drucker
and others who defined the field of modern management.
