# Marketing & Brand Strategy

## Overview

Marketing is the activity of creating, communicating, delivering, and
exchanging offerings that have value for customers. This subcategory covers
brand architecture and positioning theory (how brands are built and
managed), content marketing and organic growth, copywriting and conversion
optimization, and performance marketing and paid acquisition — the full
spectrum from strategic brand thinking to measurable acquisition tactics.
