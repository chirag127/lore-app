# Operations & Supply Chain

## Overview

Operations management focuses on the efficient transformation of inputs
into outputs. Supply chain management coordinates the flow of materials,
information, and money across the entire value chain. This subcategory
covers the theory of constraints and lean operations (eliminating waste,
optimizing flow), quality management (Six Sigma, statistical process
control), and the essential literature on operational excellence.
