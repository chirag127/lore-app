# Product Management

## Overview

Product management sits at the intersection of business, technology, and
user experience. It is the practice of defining what to build, why to build
it, and how to ensure it delivers value. This subcategory covers product
discovery and customer development (validating problems and solutions
before building), roadmapping and prioritization frameworks, and the
organizational practices that enable successful product teams.
