# Consultative Selling

## Overview

Consultative selling shifts the sales role from pitching products to
diagnosing problems and prescribing solutions. It emphasizes listening,
asking insightful questions, building trust, and demonstrating deep
knowledge of the customer's business. This subcategory covers needs
discovery, value articulation, handling objections, and closing techniques
rooted in understanding rather than pressure.
