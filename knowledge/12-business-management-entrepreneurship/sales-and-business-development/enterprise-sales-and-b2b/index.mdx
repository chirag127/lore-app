# Enterprise Sales & B2B

## Overview

Enterprise sales (B2B) involves selling products or services to other
businesses, often with long sales cycles, large deal sizes, and multiple
decision-makers. This subcategory covers account-based selling, managing
stakeholder relationships, procurement processes, RFPs, proof-of-concept
management, negotiation, and the sales methodologies tailored to complex
organizational buying decisions.
