# Sales & Business Development

## Overview

Sales is the engine that turns products into revenue. Business development
encompasses the strategic partnerships and channels that expand a company's
reach. This subcategory covers consultative selling (understanding customer
needs and delivering solutions), enterprise sales and B2B (long-cycle,
multi-stakeholder deals), and sales funnel management and CRM systems —
the processes and tools that make selling systematic and scalable.
