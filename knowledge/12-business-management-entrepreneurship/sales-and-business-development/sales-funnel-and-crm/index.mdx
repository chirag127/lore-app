# Sales Funnel & CRM

## Overview

The sales funnel (or pipeline) describes the stages a prospect moves
through from initial awareness to purchase. Customer Relationship Management
(CRM) systems are the tools used to track and manage these interactions.
This subcategory covers funnel metrics and conversion rates, lead scoring,
CRM implementation and best practices (Salesforce, HubSpot), pipeline
management, forecasting, and sales operations.
