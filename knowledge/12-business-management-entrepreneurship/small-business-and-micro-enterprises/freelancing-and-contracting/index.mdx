# Freelancing & Contracting

## Overview

Freelancing and contracting represent the simplest form of business
ownership — a single person selling their skills and time. This subcategory
covers the practical realities: finding clients, setting rates and
negotiating contracts, managing projects, invoicing and collecting payments,
tax and legal considerations, and the transition from freelancer to
business owner.
