# Small Business & Micro-Enterprises

## Overview

Most businesses are small. This subcategory addresses the unique challenges
of very small enterprises: freelancing and contracting (finding clients,
pricing, managing a solo practice), micro-agency operations (building a
small service business with a handful of people), and local business
development (retail, restaurants, services in a physical community). It
bridges the gap between personal hustle and institutional business.
