# Local Business Development

## Overview

Local businesses — retail stores, restaurants, cafes, gyms, salons, home
services — form the backbone of most economies. This subcategory covers the
unique aspects of local business: location selection, local marketing (SEO,
social media, community engagement), hiring and managing hourly employees,
regulatory compliance (licenses, health codes), and competing with chains
and e-commerce.
