# Micro-Agency Operations

## Overview

A micro-agency is a small service business typically run by 1-5 people,
offering specialized services (design, development, consulting, marketing).
This subcategory covers the unique challenges: hiring and managing a tiny
team, client acquisition and retention, project delivery at small scale,
pricing models (retainers, project-based, value-based), and the path from
freelancer to agency owner.
