# Carbon Markets & Environmental Pricing

## Overview

Environmental pricing puts a price on pollution and natural resource use to
internalize externalities. This subcategory covers the design of carbon
markets (EU ETS, California cap-and-trade), carbon taxes, carbon offsets
and credits, and the economic theory behind pricing environmental goods.
It examines the effectiveness, efficiency, and political economy of
different pricing mechanisms.
