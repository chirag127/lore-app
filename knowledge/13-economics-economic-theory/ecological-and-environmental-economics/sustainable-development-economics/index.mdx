# Sustainable Development Economics

## Overview

Sustainable development integrates economic, social, and environmental
goals. This subcategory covers the Brundtland framework, the economics of
sustainability (weak vs. strong sustainability, natural capital, genuine
savings), the Sustainable Development Goals (SDGs), green growth vs.
de-growth debates, and the measurement of well-being beyond GDP.
