# History of Economic Thought

## Overview

Economic ideas have a history. This subcategory traces the development of
economic theory from its origins to the present day. It covers the
classical school (Smith, Ricardo, Marx, Mill), the marginalist revolution,
the neoclassical synthesis, Keynesian economics, the monetarist
counterrevolution, and the development of modern heterodox schools
(Austrian, post-Keynesian, institutional, feminist economics).
