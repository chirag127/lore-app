# Globalization & Its Critics

## Overview

Globalization — the increasing integration of economies through trade,
capital flows, and migration — has transformed the world economy. This
subcategory examines the economic evidence on globalization's effects:
winners and losers, inequality, cultural impacts, global supply chains, and
the backlash against globalization in the form of populism, protectionism,
and calls for deglobalization or re-shoring.
