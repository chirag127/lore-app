# International Trade & Globalization

## Overview

International economics studies the flow of goods, services, capital, and
people across national borders. This subcategory covers trade theory
(Ricardian comparative advantage, Heckscher-Ohlin, new trade theory,
gravity models), trade policy and tariffs (protectionism, WTO, trade
agreements, trade wars), and globalization and its critics — the economic,
social, and political consequences of increasing global economic
integration.
