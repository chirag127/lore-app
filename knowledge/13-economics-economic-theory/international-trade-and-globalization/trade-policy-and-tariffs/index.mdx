# Trade Policy & Tariffs

## Overview

Trade policy is the set of laws and regulations that govern international
trade. This subcategory covers the instruments of trade policy (tariffs,
quotas, subsidies, non-tariff barriers), the World Trade Organization and
multilateral trade governance, preferential trade agreements (FTAs,
customs unions), and the political economy of trade protection and trade
wars.
