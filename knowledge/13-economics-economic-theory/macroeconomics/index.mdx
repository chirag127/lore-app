# Macroeconomics

## Overview

Macroeconomics examines aggregate economic phenomena: the total output of
goods and services, the overall price level, employment and unemployment,
and economic growth. It provides the framework for understanding fiscal
and monetary policy, business cycles, and the long-run determinants of
prosperity. This subcategory covers fiscal policy (government spending,
taxation, public debt) and monetary policy (central banking, interest rates,
money supply, inflation targeting).
