# Specific Languages

## Overview

This subcategory examines particular languages as objects of study in their own right, including their historical development, grammatical structure, literary traditions, and cultural significance. It covers English language history and usage (from Old English to global English), Sanskrit and Pali (the classical languages of Indian religion and philosophy), and major world languages (their structures, histories, and roles in global communication).
