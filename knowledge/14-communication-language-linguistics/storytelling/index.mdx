# Storytelling

## Overview

Storytelling is the ancient art of structuring experience into narrative form. This subcategory covers narrative theory (what makes a story, the grammar of narrative, types of plots), story structure (the hero's journey, three-act structure, Freytag's pyramid), and the application of narrative techniques in branding, marketing, and corporate communication. Storytelling bridges the craft of writing, the psychology of persuasion, and the anthropology of human meaning-making.
