# Writing Craft & Nonfiction Writing

## Overview

Writing craft encompasses the techniques and habits that produce clear, compelling nonfiction prose. This subcategory covers style and voice (Strunk and White, Orwell, Zinsser), narrative structure for nonfiction (the lede, the nut graph, the anecdote), journalistic and expository writing, the writing process (generating ideas, drafting, revising, editing), and the writer's discipline (routine, feedback, resilience). It draws on the tradition of great writing teachers and the accumulated wisdom of publishing.
