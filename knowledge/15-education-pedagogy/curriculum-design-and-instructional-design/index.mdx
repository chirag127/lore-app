# Curriculum Design & Instructional Design

## Overview

Curriculum design addresses what should be taught (the selection and organization of content, the sequence of learning, the scope of the curriculum). Instructional design focuses on how it should be taught (the methods, media, and activities through which learning occurs). Major instructional design models include ADDIE, Understanding by Design (Wiggins & McTighe), and Gagné's nine events of instruction. Assessment design — formative and summative, rubrics, authentic assessment — ensures that learning outcomes are validly measured.
