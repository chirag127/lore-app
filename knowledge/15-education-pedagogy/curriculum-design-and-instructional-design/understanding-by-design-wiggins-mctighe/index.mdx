*Understanding by Design* by Grant Wiggins and Jay McTighe (ASCD, 2005, expanded 2nd edition, ISBN 978-0-13-195084-9) is the foundational text of the backward design movement in K-12 and higher education. Originally published in 1998 and substantially revised in 2005, the book challenges the default "coverage model" of teaching—where instructors march through a textbook sequentially—by offering a rigorous, evidence-based alternative that begins with what students should understand and be able to do. It blends practical design tools with deep philosophical inquiry into the nature of understanding itself, making it simultaneously a practitioner's handbook and a contribution to curriculum theory.
