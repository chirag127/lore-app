# Educational Technology

## Overview

Educational technology (EdTech) examines how digital tools and platforms can enhance teaching and learning. It covers e-learning and online education (MOOCs, learning management systems, virtual classrooms), adaptive learning systems (personalized learning paths based on learner data), and artificial intelligence in education (intelligent tutoring systems, automated essay scoring, AI-assisted content creation). The field balances enthusiasm for innovation with critical assessment of effectiveness and equity.
