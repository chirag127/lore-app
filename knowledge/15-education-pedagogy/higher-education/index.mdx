# Higher Education

## Overview

Higher education examines the institutions that provide advanced learning beyond secondary schooling. It covers the history and philosophy of the university (from medieval studia to modern research universities), university systems and governance (funding models, accreditation, academic freedom), academic culture (faculty roles, tenure, disciplinary communities), and contemporary challenges (rising costs, access and equity, the impact of online learning, the role of research in society).
