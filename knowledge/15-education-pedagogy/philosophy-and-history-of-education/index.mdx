# Philosophy & History of Education

## Overview

This subcategory examines the fundamental questions of education: what is the purpose of education? What should be taught, and why? How should teaching relate to the learner's nature and development? It covers classical theories of education (Plato, Rousseau, Dewey, Montessori), the history of formal schooling (from ancient academies to modern mass education), and critical pedagogy (Freire, hooks, Giroux), which examines education's role in reproducing or challenging social inequality.
