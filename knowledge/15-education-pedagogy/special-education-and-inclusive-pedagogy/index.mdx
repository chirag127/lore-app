# Special Education & Inclusive Pedagogy

## Overview

Special education addresses the needs of students with disabilities, learning differences, and exceptionalities. Inclusive pedagogy extends beyond special education to embrace the principle that all learners can participate in general education classrooms with appropriate supports. Key frameworks include Universal Design for Learning (UDL), differentiated instruction, response to intervention (RTI), and individualized education programs (IEPs). This subcategory covers both the legal frameworks and the practical teaching strategies.
