title: "Anthropology"
parent: "17-social-sciences"
order: 1
description: "The holistic study of human beings across time, space, and culture."
---

Anthropology examines what it means to be human through four subfields: cultural anthropology (ethnographic study of living societies), linguistic anthropology (language as cultural practice), physical/biological anthropology (human evolution and variation), and archaeology (past societies through material remains). Its signature method is participant-observation fieldwork.
