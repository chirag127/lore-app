---
title: "Cultural Studies"
parent: "17-social-sciences"
order: 2
description: "The interdisciplinary analysis of culture, power, and meaning in contemporary society."
---

Cultural studies emerged from the Birmingham School (Hall, Hoggart, Williams) to analyze how culture produces and circulates meaning within power structures. It covers media studies, popular culture, postcolonial theory, subculture analysis, and the politics of representation. Rejects disciplinary boundaries in favor of problem-driven inquiry.
