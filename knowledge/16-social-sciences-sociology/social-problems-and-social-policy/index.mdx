description: "The sociological analysis of social problems and the policies designed to address them."
---

Examines how social conditions come to be defined as problems requiring intervention, and the policies societies devise in response. Covers crime and deviance, poverty and social welfare, education policy and social reproduction, and the intended and unintended consequences of policy interventions.
