parent: "17-social-sciences"

description: "The systematic study of social structure, social interaction, and social change."
---

Sociology investigates how social structures — institutions, hierarchies, networks, cultural norms — shape human action and life outcomes. Covers social theory (Marx, Durkheim, Weber), social stratification and class, organizations and institutions, and the sociology of technology and digital society. Its distinctive lens is making the familiar strange by revealing the social forces beneath everyday life.
