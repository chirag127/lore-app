Indian law is a distinct legal system combining common law heritage with indigenous statutory frameworks. The Constitution of India is the supreme law, and its interpretation shapes every area of Indian jurisprudence. This subcategory also covers India's contract and commercial law, its GST and taxation regime, and labor and employment law.

### Sub-subcategories

- **indian-constitutional-law** — Fundamental rights, directive principles, federal structure, amendment power, landmark Supreme Court cases
- **indian-contract-act-and-commercial-law** — Indian Contract Act, sale of goods, partnership, negotiable instruments
- **indian-taxation-law-and-gst** — Income tax, corporate tax, goods and services tax, transfer pricing
- **indian-labor-law-and-employment-relations** — Industrial disputes, factory laws, social security, wage codes
