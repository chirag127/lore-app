Legal practice bridges academic legal knowledge and the profession. It covers the craft skills every lawyer needs — legal research, writing, and advocacy — as well as the ethical obligations that govern the profession. Alternative dispute resolution (arbitration, mediation, negotiation) offers pathways to resolve conflicts outside traditional litigation.

### Sub-subcategories

- **legal-ethics-and-professional-responsibility** — Duties to clients, confidentiality, conflicts of interest, professional conduct rules
- **legal-research-and-writing** — Case analysis, statutory interpretation, persuasive writing, citation, briefs and memoranda
- **alternative-dispute-resolution-and-arbitration** — Arbitration law, mediation techniques, negotiation theory, enforcement of awards
