# Guns, Germs, and Steel — Jared Diamond

**Leaf:** `human-prehistory-and-evolution`
**Subcategory:** `big-history-and-civilization`

A 13,000-year investigation into why Eurasian civilizations conquered the globe — and why the answer lies in geography, not racial superiority. Diamond's Pulitzer Prize-winning synthesis of history, biology, geography, and archaeology remains the foundational text of the "big history" genre.
