Intellectual history traces the development of ideas across time — what people thought, how those thoughts changed, and what contexts shaped them. It covers the history of philosophy, political ideologies, religious doctrines, and scientific paradigms, revealing how ideas that seem timeless were born in specific historical circumstances.

### Sub-subcategories

- **history-of-philosophy-and-philosophical-movements** — Ancient philosophy, Scholasticism, Enlightenment, German idealism, existentialism, pragmatism
- **history-of-political-ideas** — Liberty, equality, rights, sovereignty, democracy, socialism, nationalism
- **history-of-religion-and-theology** — Doctrinal development, heresy, reform movements, secularization
- **history-of-science-and-scientific-revolutions** — Copernican revolution, Newtonian synthesis, Darwinian evolution, quantum revolution
