The history of technology examines how human ingenuity has transformed material life — from the steam engine to the microprocessor. It situates technological change in social, economic, and political context, asking not just how technologies developed but how they reshaped the societies that produced them.

### Sub-subcategories

- **industrial-technology-and-manufacturing** — Steam power, electricity, assembly line, chemical engineering, automation
- **history-of-computing-and-programming** — Analytical engine, ENIAC, transistors, operating systems, programming languages
- **digital-technology-and-the-internet-era** — ARPANET, world wide web, mobile computing, cloud, AI
