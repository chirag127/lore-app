Military history studies organized violence between political communities — its conduct, its causes, and its consequences. It encompasses the evolution of strategy and tactics, the lives of great commanders, the experience of ordinary soldiers, and the transformative impact of military technology on society.

### Sub-subcategories

- **history-of-warfare-and-military-strategy** — From phalanx to drone warfare, strategic theory, just war tradition
- **great-military-campaigns-and-commanders** — Alexander, Caesar, Napoleon, Grant, Rommel, Slim, and their defining campaigns
- **military-technology-and-its-societal-impact** — Gunpowder, rifling, tanks, aircraft, nuclear weapons, precision munitions
