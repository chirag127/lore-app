*Guns, Germs, and Steel* is Jared Diamond's Pulitzer Prize-winning answer to Yali's question: why did some societies develop guns, germs, and steel while others did not? Tracing 13,000 years of human history, Diamond builds a causal chain from domesticable plants and animals to continental axes to the technologies and diseases that enabled Eurasian conquest.
