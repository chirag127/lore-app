World history traces the rise and fall of civilizations from prehistory through the medieval and early modern periods. It examines how human societies organized themselves, what they believed, and how they interacted across continents before the modern era of globalization.

### Sub-subcategories

- **prehistoric-and-ancient-history** — Human origins, Neolithic revolution, Mesopotamia, Egypt, Indus Valley, early China
- **classical-civilizations** — Greece, Rome, Maurya and Gupta India, Han China, Persia, Aksum
- **medieval-and-early-modern-history** — Feudalism, Byzantium, Islamic Golden Age, Mongol Empire, Renaissance, Reformation
- **age-of-exploration-and-colonial-expansion** — European maritime empires, Columbus exchange, colonization of the Americas, early colonial economies
