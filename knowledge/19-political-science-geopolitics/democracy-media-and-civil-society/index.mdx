# Democracy, Media & Civil Society

## Overview

Democracy depends not only on formal institutions but on a healthy public sphere — including free media, vibrant civil society, and reliable electoral processes. This subcategory examines how these pillars function and the threats they face: disinformation, polarization, electoral manipulation, and the erosion of democratic norms. It also studies social movements as vehicles for democratic participation and change.
