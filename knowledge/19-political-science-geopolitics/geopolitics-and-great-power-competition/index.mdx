# Geopolitics & Great-Power Competition

## Overview

Geopolitics analyzes how geographic factors — territory, resources, strategic chokepoints — shape the competition among major powers. It draws on classical geopoliticians (Mahan, Mackinder, Spykman) and contemporary strategic studies to understand the rivalry between the United States and China, the evolving role of India, and the implications of technology, energy, and demographics for global power balances.
