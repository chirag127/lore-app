# Political Economy

## Overview

Political economy examines the reciprocal relationship between politics and economics. It analyzes how state institutions shape markets (through regulation, taxation, industrial policy) and how economic interests and classes influence political power. Key topics include the varieties of capitalism, the welfare state, state-led development, and the political economy of developing nations.
