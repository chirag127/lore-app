# Political Theory & Philosophy

## Overview

Political theory examines the foundational concepts and justifications of political life. It asks what makes government legitimate, what justice requires, what freedom means, and how authority should be structured. Drawing on the history of political thought and contemporary analytic philosophy, this subcategory provides the normative and conceptual framework for all other political science inquiry.
