# Public Policy & Governance

## Overview

Public policy studies how problems come to be defined, how solutions are designed and adopted, and how policies are implemented and evaluated. Governance extends beyond government to include the networks, regulations, and norms through which collective decisions are made. This subcategory covers regulatory theory, administrative law, the policy cycle, and emerging challenges of digital governance and technology policy.
