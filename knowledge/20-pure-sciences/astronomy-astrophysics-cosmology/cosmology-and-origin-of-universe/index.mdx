# Cosmology & Origin of Universe

## Overview

Cosmology addresses the biggest questions: How did the universe begin? What
is it made of? How will it end? This subcategory covers the Big Bang theory,
cosmic inflation, the cosmic microwave background, the large-scale structure
of the universe, and the mysterious components — dark matter and dark energy
— that dominate the cosmos.
