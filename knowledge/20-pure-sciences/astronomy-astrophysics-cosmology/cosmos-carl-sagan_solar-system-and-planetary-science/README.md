# Cosmos — Carl Sagan (1980)

**Category:** 20-pure-sciences / astronomy-astrophysics-cosmology / solar-system-and-planetary-science

The companion book to the landmark PBS television series, _Cosmos_ became the best-selling science book ever published in English, spending 70 weeks on the New York Times bestseller list. It won the Hugo Award for Best Non-Fiction Book in 1981 and was designated by the Library of Congress as one of 88 books "that shaped America."
