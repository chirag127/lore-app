# Astronomy, Astrophysics & Cosmology

## Overview

Astronomy observes celestial objects and phenomena beyond Earth's
atmosphere. Astrophysics applies the laws of physics to understand these
objects. Cosmology studies the universe as a whole — its origin, structure,
and ultimate fate. This subcategory covers stellar astrophysics and galaxy
formation, solar system and planetary science, and cosmology including the
Big Bang, cosmic inflation, dark matter, and dark energy.
