# Solar System & Planetary Science

## Overview

Planetary science explores the formation, structure, and evolution of
planets and other bodies in our solar system and beyond. It draws on
geology, atmospheric science, and astrophysics to understand worlds as
diverse as Venus, Mars, Jupiter, and the icy moons of the outer solar
system. Exoplanetary science extends this to planets orbiting other stars.
