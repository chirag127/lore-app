# Stellar Astrophysics & Galaxy Formation

## Overview

Stellar astrophysics studies the life cycle of stars: from gravitational
collapse and nuclear fusion through main sequence evolution to white
dwarfs, neutron stars, and black holes. Galaxy formation tracks how
billions of stars assemble into galaxies and how galaxies evolve and
interact over cosmic time.
