# Genome: The Autobiography of a Species in 23 Chapters

**Author:** Matt Ridley | **Year:** 1999 | **Pages:** 344 | **ISBN:** 9780060894085

Book #2 in the genetics-and-genomics leaf. A chromosome-by-chromosome tour
of the human genome, published at the moment the Human Genome Project was
completing its first draft. Each of the 23 chapters examines a single
chromosome and uses it as a lens on a different aspect of human nature —
from the origins of life to the question of free will.
