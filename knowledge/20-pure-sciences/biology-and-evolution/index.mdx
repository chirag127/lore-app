# Biology & Evolution

## Overview

Biology is the study of living organisms — how they function, evolve,
reproduce, and interact. This subcategory covers the core pillars:
evolutionary biology and natural selection (the unifying theory of biology),
genetics and genomics (the molecular basis of inheritance), and the
mechanisms of evolution including adaptation, speciation, and the tree of
life.
