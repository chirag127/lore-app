# Biochemistry & Molecular Chemistry

## Overview

Biochemistry is the chemistry of life. It studies the structure and function
of biomolecules: proteins, nucleic acids, carbohydrates, and lipids. It
covers enzyme catalysis, metabolic pathways, signal transduction, and the
molecular basis of genetics. This subcategory bridges chemistry and biology
and is fundamental to medicine, biotechnology, and molecular biology.
