# Chemistry

## Overview

Chemistry is the central science, bridging physics and biology. It studies
the composition, structure, and properties of substances and the
transformations they undergo. This subcategory covers physical chemistry
and thermochemistry (thermodynamics, kinetics, quantum chemistry),
inorganic and organic chemistry (reactions, mechanisms, synthesis), and
biochemistry and molecular chemistry (the chemistry of life).
