# Inorganic & Organic Chemistry

## Overview

Organic chemistry studies carbon-containing compounds — the chemistry of
life, plastics, pharmaceuticals, and fuels. Inorganic chemistry covers the
chemistry of all other elements, including metals, minerals, coordination
compounds, and organometallics. Both fields are essential for understanding
molecular structure, reactivity, and synthesis.
