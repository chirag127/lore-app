# Napoleon's Buttons: How 17 Molecules Changed History

**Authors:** Penny Le Couteur & Jay Burreson
**Year:** 2003
**Category:** 20-pure-sciences / chemistry
**ISBN:** 9781585423316

## Summary

A fascinating exploration of 17 molecules or molecular families that shaped human history — from the spices that drove the Age of Exploration to the quinine that made tropical colonization possible. Each chapter reveals how the precise arrangement of atoms in a molecule determined its properties and, through those properties, influenced the course of wars, empires, medicine, and daily life.
