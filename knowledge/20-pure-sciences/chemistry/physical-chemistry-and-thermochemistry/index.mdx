# Physical Chemistry & Thermochemistry

## Overview

Physical chemistry uses the principles of physics to understand chemical
systems. It covers chemical thermodynamics (energy changes in reactions),
chemical kinetics (reaction rates and mechanisms), quantum chemistry (the
electronic structure of atoms and molecules), and spectroscopy (the
interaction of matter with electromagnetic radiation).
