# The Disappearing Spoon

**Author:** Sam Kean
**Year:** 2010
**Category:** 20-pure-sciences / chemistry
**ISBN:** 9780316051637

## Summary

A narrative journey through the periodic table, exploring the bizarre, tragic, and inspiring stories behind each element. From Marie Curie's radium to Fritz Haber's poison gas to gallium spoons that melt in hot tea, Sam Kean reveals the human drama behind the familiar wall chart.
