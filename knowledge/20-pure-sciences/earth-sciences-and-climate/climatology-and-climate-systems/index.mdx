# Climatology & Climate Systems

## Overview

Climatology studies the long-term patterns of temperature, precipitation,
and atmospheric circulation that define Earth's climate. It covers the
physical drivers of climate (solar radiation, greenhouse gases, aerosols,
albedo), climate feedbacks, climate modeling, and the evidence for and
impacts of anthropogenic climate change.
