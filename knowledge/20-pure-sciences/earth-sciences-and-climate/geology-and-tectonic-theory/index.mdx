# Geology & Tectonic Theory

## Overview

Geology studies the solid Earth — its composition, structure, and the
processes that have shaped it over billions of years. Plate tectonics is the
unifying theory that explains earthquakes, volcanoes, mountain building, and
the distribution of continents and oceans. This subcategory also covers
mineralogy, petrology, seismology, and Earth's deep interior.
