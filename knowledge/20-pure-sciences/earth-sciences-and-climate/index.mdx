# Earth Sciences & Climate

## Overview

Earth sciences study the physical and chemical processes that shape our
planet. This subcategory covers geology and tectonic theory (plate
tectonics, mineralogy, petrology, seismology), oceanography and marine
science (ocean circulation, marine ecosystems, seafloor geology), and
climatology and climate systems (atmospheric physics, climate modeling,
the greenhouse effect, and climate change).
