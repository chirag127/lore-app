# Oceanography & Marine Science

## Overview

Oceanography explores the oceans that cover 71% of Earth's surface. It is
an interdisciplinary science: physical oceanography studies currents, waves,
and ocean circulation; chemical oceanography examines seawater composition
and biogeochemical cycles; biological oceanography investigates marine
ecosystems; and geological oceanography studies the seafloor.
