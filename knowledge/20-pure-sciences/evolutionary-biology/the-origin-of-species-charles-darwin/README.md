# On the Origin of Species — Charles Darwin (1859)

The foundational text of evolutionary biology. Darwin argues that species evolve through natural selection acting on heritable variation, and supports this thesis with evidence from domestication, the fossil record, biogeography, comparative anatomy, and embryology.
