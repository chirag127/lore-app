# Cognitive Neuroscience & Decision Making

## Overview

Cognitive neuroscience seeks to understand how brain processes give rise
to mental functions. This subcategory covers the neural basis of perception,
attention, memory, language, executive function, and decision-making. It
bridges psychology and neuroscience, using brain imaging (fMRI, EEG),
lesion studies, and computational modeling to understand cognition.
