# Neuroscience

## Overview

Neuroscience is the interdisciplinary study of the nervous system. It spans
multiple levels of analysis from molecular and cellular mechanisms to
systems, cognition, and behavior. This subcategory covers neuroanatomy and
brain architecture (the structural organization of the brain), cognitive
neuroscience (perception, memory, decision-making, consciousness), and
neuropharmacology and behavioral neuroscience (how drugs affect the nervous
system and behavior).
