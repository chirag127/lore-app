# Neuropharmacology & Behavioral Neuroscience

## Overview

Behavioral neuroscience examines the neural mechanisms underlying behavior
— from simple reflexes to complex motivated behaviors like feeding,
sleeping, and social interaction. Neuropharmacology studies how drugs
(psychoactive, therapeutic, recreational) alter neural function and
behavior. This subcategory bridges molecular neuroscience, psychology, and
psychiatry.
