# The Man Who Mistook His Wife for a Hat and Other Clinical Tales

**Author:** Oliver Sacks  
**Year:** 1985  
**Category:** 20-pure-sciences / neuroscience  
**ISBN:** 9780593466674 (current paperback)

## Summary

A collection of 24 case studies of patients with extraordinary neurological disorders — visual agnosia, amnesia, Tourette's syndrome, autism savant syndrome, proprioception loss, and many others — each revealing something profound about how the brain constructs reality, identity, and consciousness.
