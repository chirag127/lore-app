**Why this book?** Unconventional/contrarian pick. Where Kuhn argued that science changes through paradigm shifts, Feyerabend went further: there is no method at all. "Anything goes." This book is the radical counterpart to The Structure of Scientific Revolutions — the anarchist to Kuhn's revolutionary.
