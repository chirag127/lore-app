# History of Scientific Revolutions

## Overview

Science progresses through revolutions as much as through steady
accumulation. This subcategory covers the great transformations in
scientific understanding: the Copernican revolution, the Newtonian
synthesis, the Darwinian revolution in biology, the quantum revolution, the
discovery of DNA and the molecular biology revolution, and the
computational and AI revolutions in science.
