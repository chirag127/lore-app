# Philosophy & History of Science

## Overview

Science is not just a body of knowledge — it is a process with a history
and a philosophy. This subcategory examines the scientific method itself:
how theories are confirmed, falsified, and replaced. It covers the history
of scientific revolutions (Copernican, Darwinian, quantum, etc.), the
philosophy of physics, biology, and mind (reductionism, emergence, the
nature of laws), and the concept of paradigm shifts in scientific progress.
