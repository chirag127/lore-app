# Scientific Method & Paradigm Shifts

## Overview

This subcategory examines the methodology of science: what distinguishes
science from non-science (demarcation), how theories are confirmed or
falsified, and how scientific revolutions occur. Key figures include Karl
Popper (falsificationism), Thomas Kuhn (paradigm shifts and normal
science), Paul Feyerabend (epistemological anarchism), and Imre Lakatos
(research programmes).
