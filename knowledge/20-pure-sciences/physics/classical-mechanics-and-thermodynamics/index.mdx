# Classical Mechanics & Thermodynamics

## Overview

Classical mechanics describes the motion of bodies under the action of
forces, from projectiles to planetary orbits. Thermodynamics studies heat,
work, and the statistical behavior of large ensembles of particles. Together
they form the foundation of macroscopic physics and the launching point for
quantum and relativistic theories.
