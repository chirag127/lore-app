# Condensed Matter Physics

## Overview

Condensed matter physics explores the behavior of materials in their solid
and liquid phases. It explains why some materials conduct electricity while
others insulate, why some become superconducting, and how crystals form.
This is the largest branch of contemporary physics and the foundation for
semiconductor technology and materials science.
