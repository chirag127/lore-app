# Electromagnetism & Optics

## Overview

Electromagnetism unifies electricity, magnetism, and light through
Maxwell's equations. Optics studies the behavior of light — reflection,
refraction, diffraction, polarization. This field underlies most modern
technology from radio to lasers to fiber-optic communications.
