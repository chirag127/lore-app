# Physics

## Overview

Physics seeks to understand the fundamental laws that govern the universe,
from the smallest subatomic particles to the largest cosmic structures. It
uses mathematics as its language and experiment as its arbiter. This
subcategory covers classical mechanics and thermodynamics, electromagnetism
and optics, quantum mechanics and quantum field theory, relativity,
particle physics and the Standard Model, and condensed matter physics.
