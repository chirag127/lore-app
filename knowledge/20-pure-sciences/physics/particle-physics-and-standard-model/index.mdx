# Particle Physics & Standard Model

## Overview

Particle physics seeks the most elementary building blocks of the universe
and the fundamental forces through which they interact. The Standard Model
of particle physics classifies all known elementary particles (quarks,
leptons, bosons) and describes three of the four fundamental forces. It
is one of the most precisely tested theories in science.
