# Quantum Mechanics & Quantum Field Theory

## Overview

Quantum mechanics describes nature at its smallest scales, where particles
behave as waves and probabilities replace certainties. Quantum field theory
extends quantum mechanics to relativistic systems and provides the
framework for the Standard Model of particle physics. Key concepts include
wave functions, superposition, entanglement, and quantization of fields.
