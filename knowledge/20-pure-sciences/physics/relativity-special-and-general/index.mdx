# Relativity — Special & General

## Overview

Special relativity unified space and time into spacetime and established
the constancy of the speed of light. General relativity describes gravity
as the curvature of spacetime by mass and energy. These theories have
profound implications for cosmology, black holes, gravitational waves, and
the nature of time itself.
