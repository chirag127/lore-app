# Something Deeply Hidden — Sean M. Carroll (2019)

## Quantum Worlds and the Emergence of Spacetime

**Slug:** `something-deeply-hidden-sean-carroll`  
**Leaf:** `20-pure-sciences/physics/quantum-mechanics-and-quantum-field-theory`

A bold, lucid defense of the Many-Worlds Interpretation of quantum mechanics. Carroll argues that the universe is a wave function evolving by the Schrodinger equation, with no collapse — only branching via decoherence — and that spacetime itself emerges from quantum entanglement.
