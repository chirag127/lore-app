# Popular Science & Science Communication

## Overview

Science communication is the bridge between specialized research and public
understanding. This subcategory collects works that explain scientific ideas
to a general audience with clarity, narrative skill, and intellectual
honesty. It includes books by leading scientists writing for the public,
science journalism that illuminates current research, and writing that
conveys the wonder and rigor of scientific discovery.
