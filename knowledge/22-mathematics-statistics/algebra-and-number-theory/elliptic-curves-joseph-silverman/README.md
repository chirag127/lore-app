## The Arithmetic of Elliptic Curves

The standard graduate-level introduction to the arithmetic theory of elliptic curves, covering the geometry, formal groups, and arithmetic over finite, complex, local, and global fields. Proves the Mordell-Weil theorem and Siegel's theorem.
