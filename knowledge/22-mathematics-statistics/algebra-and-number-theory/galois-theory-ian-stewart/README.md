# Galois Theory — Ian Stewart (1973)

**ISBN-13:** 9780412108006 | **Pages:** 226 | **Publisher:** Chapman and Hall, London

The first undergraduate textbook on Galois theory. Stewart presents the subject as a showpiece of mathematical unification, bringing together group theory and field theory to prove that the general quintic equation is unsolvable by radicals.
