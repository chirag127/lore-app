Kuldeep Singh's Number Theory: Step by Step fills a genuine gap in the textbook market: a genuinely patient, carefully scaffolded introduction for students who need more guidance than traditional texts provide. Published by Oxford University Press in 2020, the book reflects modern pedagogical thinking about how students learn mathematics.

The text moves from the simplest ideas — divisors, multiples, and the division algorithm — through congruences, prime numbers, and quadratic reciprocity, to applications in cryptography including the RSA and ElGamal cryptosystems. Each concept is introduced with a clear statement, followed by fully worked examples, then by exercises with solutions. The "step by step" in the title is literal: every computation is broken down into numbered steps, making the reasoning transparent.
