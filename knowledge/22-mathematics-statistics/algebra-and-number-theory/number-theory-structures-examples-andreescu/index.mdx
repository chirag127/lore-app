Titu Andreescu and Dorin Andrica's Number Theory: Structures, Examples, and Problems is the definitive problem-solving guide for olympiad-level number theory. Andreescu, the long-time director of the American Mathematics Competitions and coach of the US IMO team, brings unparalleled expertise in competitive mathematics.

The book is organized not by mathematical chronology but by problem structures: divisibility, primes, congruences, quadratic residues, Diophantine equations, and integer sequences. Each chapter provides a concise theoretical overview followed by dozens of carefully chosen examples and hundreds of problems with complete solutions. The approach is systematic and methodical, designed to build the pattern recognition skills essential for competition success.
