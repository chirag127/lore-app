# Logic & Foundations of Computation

This leaf covers the foundational logical results that underpin all of computer science — Gödel's incompleteness theorems, formal axiomatic systems, the relationship between mathematics and meta-mathematics, and the limits of formal reasoning. These are the results that defined what computation can and cannot achieve.
