# Theory of Computation

## Overview

Theory of computation asks the most fundamental questions in computer
science: what problems can be solved by computation, and how efficiently?
This subcategory covers automata theory (finite automata, pushdown automata,
Turing machines), formal languages and grammars (regular, context-free),
computability and decidability (the halting problem, undecidability), and
computational complexity classes (P, NP, PSPACE, and beyond).

## Books

- [Gödel, Escher, Bach: An Eternal Golden Braid](godel-escher-bach-douglas-hofstadter) — Douglas R. Hofstadter (1979) — A Pulitzer Prize-winning exploration of self-reference, strange loops, and the nature of consciousness through mathematics, art, and music.
