# DevOps, SRE & Platform Engineering

## Overview

DevOps is a cultural and technical movement that breaks down silos between
development and operations. Site Reliability Engineering (SRE) applies
software engineering practices to operations problems. Platform engineering
builds internal developer platforms that enable engineering teams to ship
faster. This subcategory covers CI/CD pipelines, container orchestration
(Kubernetes), infrastructure as code, monitoring and observability, incident
management and on-call practices, and the principles of site reliability.
