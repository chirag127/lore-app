# Engineering Culture & Team Dynamics

## Overview

Great software is built by great teams. Engineering culture encompasses the
norms, practices, and values that shape how engineers work together. This
subcategory covers technical leadership and staff engineering, engineering
productivity and developer experience, managing technical debt, working on
remote engineering teams, and the organizational patterns that enable
effective software development at scale.
