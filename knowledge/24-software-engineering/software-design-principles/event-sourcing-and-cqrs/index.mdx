---
title: "Event Sourcing & CQRS"
parent: "24-software-engineering/software-design-principles"
order: 3
description: "Patterns for persisting state as a sequence of events."
---

Event sourcing stores state changes as an immutable log. CQRS separates read and write models. Together they enable audit trails, temporal queries, and complex event-driven workflows at the cost of operational complexity.
