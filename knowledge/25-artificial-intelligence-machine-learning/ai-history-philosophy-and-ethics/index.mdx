# AI History, Philosophy & Ethics

## Overview

AI is not just a technical discipline — it raises profound questions about
mind, intelligence, and the future of society. This subcategory covers the
history of artificial intelligence from its origins to the present day,
philosophical questions about machine consciousness and the distinction
between strong and weak AI, ethics of AI decision-making, and the
societal impacts of automation on labor, inequality, and power.
