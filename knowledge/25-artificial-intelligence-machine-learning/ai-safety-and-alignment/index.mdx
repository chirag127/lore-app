# AI Safety & Alignment

## Overview

As AI systems become more capable, ensuring they behave safely and as
intended becomes critical. This subcategory covers value alignment and the
control problem (how to specify and instill human values in AI),
interpretability and explainability (understanding what models learn and
why they make decisions), adversarial robustness and red teaming, and AI
governance and regulation frameworks.
