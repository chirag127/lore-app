# Deep Learning & Neural Architectures

## Overview

Deep learning uses multi-layer neural networks to learn representations
from data. This subcategory covers the major architectural families:
feedforward networks and backpropagation, convolutional neural networks
for visual data, recurrent networks and LSTMs for sequences, transformers
and attention mechanisms, and generative models (GANs, VAEs, diffusion
models, autoregressive models).
