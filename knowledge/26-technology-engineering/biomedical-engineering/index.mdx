# Biomedical Engineering

## Overview

Biomedical engineering applies engineering methods to solve problems in
medicine and biology. It builds bridges between the engineering and life
sciences. This subcategory covers bioinformatics and computational biology
(analyzing biological data with computational tools), medical device design
(from imaging systems to implants), and neural engineering and prosthetics
(brain-computer interfaces, neural stimulation, artificial limbs).
