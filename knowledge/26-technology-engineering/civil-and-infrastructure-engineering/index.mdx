# Civil & Infrastructure Engineering

## Overview

Civil engineering shapes the physical world we inhabit — buildings, bridges,
roads, dams, water systems. This subcategory covers structural engineering
and construction (materials, analysis, design of structures), transportation
systems and urban infrastructure (highways, railways, airports, transit),
and water resources and hydraulics (hydrology, water distribution, flood
control).
