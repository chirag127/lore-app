# Electrical & Electronics Engineering

## Overview

Electrical engineering deals with the generation, transmission, and
utilization of electrical energy. Electronics engineering focuses on
circuits and systems using semiconductor devices. This subcategory covers
circuit theory and electronic design, embedded systems and microcontrollers,
power systems and electrical infrastructure, and signal processing and
communications engineering.
