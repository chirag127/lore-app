# Energy & Sustainability Engineering

## Overview

Energy engineering addresses one of humanity's most critical challenges:
providing reliable, affordable, and sustainable energy. This subcategory
covers renewable energy systems (solar, wind, hydro, geothermal,
bioenergy), nuclear engineering (fission, fusion, reactor design, safety),
and energy storage and grid systems (batteries, hydrogen, grid integration,
smart grids).
