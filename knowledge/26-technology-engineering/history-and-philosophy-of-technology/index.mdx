# History & Philosophy of Technology

## Overview

Technology is not neutral — it shapes and is shaped by society. This
subcategory examines the history of technological change, from the
Industrial Revolution to the digital age, and asks philosophical questions
about the relationship between humans and their tools. It covers the
industrial revolutions and technological transitions, philosophies of
technology (determinism, instrumentalism, critical theory of technology),
and speculation about the future of technology.
