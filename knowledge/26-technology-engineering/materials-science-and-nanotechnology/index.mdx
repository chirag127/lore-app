# Materials Science & Nanotechnology

## Overview

Materials science investigates the structure-property relationships of
materials and engineers them for specific applications. This subcategory
covers semiconductor materials and manufacturing (the foundation of modern
electronics), polymer science and composite materials (plastics, elastomers,
fiber-reinforced composites), and nanomaterials and nanofabrication
(manipulating matter at the atomic and molecular scale).
