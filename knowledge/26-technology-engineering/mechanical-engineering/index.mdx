# Mechanical Engineering

## Overview

Mechanical engineering is one of the broadest engineering disciplines,
concerned with the design and operation of machines and mechanical systems.
It is the engineering of motion, force, energy, and materials. This
subcategory covers classical mechanics and structural analysis,
thermodynamics and heat transfer, and manufacturing processes and
industrial design.
