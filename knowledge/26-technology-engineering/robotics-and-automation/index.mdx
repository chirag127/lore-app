# Robotics & Automation

## Overview

Robotics integrates mechanical engineering, electrical engineering, and
computer science to create machines that can perceive their environment and
act upon it. This subcategory covers autonomous systems and motion planning,
drone technology and autonomous vehicles, and industrial robotics and
human-robot interaction — the full spectrum from factory floor to self-
driving cars.
