The creative life is a subject of endless fascination. This subcategory collects biographies of artists (Picasso, van Gogh, da Vinci), writers (Shakespeare, Tolstoy, Woolf, Borges), and musicians (Beethoven, Miles Davis, Bob Dylan) that explore the relationship between creative output and lived experience.
