Some of the most illuminating life-writing examines multiple figures together, revealing patterns, networks, and shared contexts that single biographies miss. This subcategory covers Plutarch's Parallel Lives, group biographies of movements and schools, prosopography (the study of groups through their common characteristics), and works that trace an idea or institution through the lives of its key figures.
