Distinct from technology founders (who may be covered under that subcategory) and investors, this subcategory focuses on entrepreneurs and business leaders who built companies across all industries — from Henry Ford and Ray Kroc to Anita Roddick and Jack Ma. These biographies illuminate the practical challenges of building, scaling, and sustaining organizations.
