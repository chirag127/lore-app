The worlds of finance and investing have produced outsized characters whose stories illuminate the workings of capital, the psychology of markets, and the nature of risk and reward. This subcategory covers biographies of figures from J.P. Morgan and John D. Rockefeller to Warren Buffett, George Soros, and Charlie Munger.
