From Galileo and Newton to Einstein, Feynman, Curie, and Darwin, the history of science is a history of individuals who saw what others did not. This subcategory collects biographies that reveal the intellectual journeys, personal struggles, and social contexts of the world's greatest scientists and mathematicians.
