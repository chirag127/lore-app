From Ada Lovelace to Steve Jobs, from Grace Hopper to Linus Torvalds, the technology industry has been shaped by remarkable individuals whose stories reveal the human side of innovation. This subcategory collects biographies of founders, engineers, and technology leaders whose work transformed how we live, work, and communicate.

## Books

- **The Innovators: How a Group of Hackers, Geniuses, and Geeks Created the Digital Revolution** — Walter Isaacson (2014) — A sweeping history of the digital revolution, tracing collaborative innovation from Ada Lovelace to the World Wide Web.
