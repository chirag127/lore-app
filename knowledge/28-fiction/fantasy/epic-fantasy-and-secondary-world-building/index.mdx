The epic fantasy tradition, defined by Tolkien's Middle-earth, creates secondary worlds of immense depth and detail. This subcategory covers the architects of imagined worlds: Tolkien, Le Guin, Jordan, Martin, Hobb, and others who build universes that feel as real as our own, with their own histories, cultures, mythologies, and rules of magic.
