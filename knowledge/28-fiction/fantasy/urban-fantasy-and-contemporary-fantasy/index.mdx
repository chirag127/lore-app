Urban fantasy brings the magical into the mundane: vampires in New York, wizards in London, gods walking among us. From Susanna Clarke's Jonathan Strange & Mr Norrell to Neil Gaiman's American Gods and J.K. Rowling's Harry Potter, this tradition explores what happens when the supernatural intersects with the recognizable everyday world.
