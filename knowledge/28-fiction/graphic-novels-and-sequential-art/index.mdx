The graphic novel is a hybrid form in which meaning emerges from the interplay of word and image, panel and gutter, page turn and composition. From comic strips and superhero comics to the sophisticated literary graphic novel (Maus, Persepolis, Watchmen), the medium has proven capable of extraordinary range and depth. This subcategory covers the theory, history, and practice of sequential art narrative.
