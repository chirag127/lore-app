Mystery and crime fiction is the most structurally disciplined of the popular genres, built around the revelation of a hidden truth — usually a crime and its perpetrator. From the classical puzzle of the whodunnit to the psychological thriller's exploration of motive and mind, from hard-boiled detective fiction to the immersive detail of true crime narrative, this genre offers both intellectual satisfaction and moral exploration.

## Sub-Subcategories

- **[Detective Fiction](detective-fiction)** — The puzzle narrative centered on the detective figure.
- **[Psychological Thriller](psychological-thriller)** — Suspense driven by psychology, perception, and unreliable narrators.
- **[True Crime Narrative](true-crime-narrative)** — Non-fiction crime storytelling with novelistic intensity.
