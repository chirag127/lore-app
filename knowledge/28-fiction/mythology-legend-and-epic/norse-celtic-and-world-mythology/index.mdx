Beyond the classical and Indian traditions, every culture has its foundational stories. This subcategory covers Norse mythology (the Eddas, the sagas), Celtic mythology (the Mabinogion, Irish myth cycles), and the mythologies of Mesopotamia, Egypt, China, Japan, Africa, the Americas, and the Pacific Islands — the rich diversity of human storytelling across time and place.
