From Asimov's robot stories to the Turing test dilemmas of Dick and the post-human landscapes of contemporary SF, this subcategory collects works in which technology — especially AI, robotics, and digital consciousness — is the central subject and driver of the narrative. These stories ask: what does it mean to be human when machines think? What happens to identity, morality, and society when technology outpaces biology?
