title: 'Hard Science Fiction'
parent: '28-fiction/science-fiction'
order: 3
description: 'Science fiction that grounds its speculation in rigorous physics, biology, and engineering.'
---

Hard SF takes the "science" of science fiction seriously, building its worlds from the known laws of nature and extrapolating forward with scientific plausibility. Key figures include Arthur C. Clarke, Kim Stanley Robinson, Greg Egan, and Andy Weir. The pleasure of hard SF lies in the intellectual puzzle of making the impossible seem possible within the constraints of real science.
