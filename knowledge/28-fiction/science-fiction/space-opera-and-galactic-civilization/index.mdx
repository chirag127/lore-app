Space opera takes the epic scope of Homer and transfers it to the stars: galactic empires, interstellar wars, alien civilizations, and heroes whose actions shape the fate of worlds. From E.E. Smith's Lensman series to Frank Herbert's Dune, Iain M. Banks's Culture novels, and modern expansive series, space opera delivers the pleasures of the epic in a science-fictional key.
