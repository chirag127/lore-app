Drama is literature designed for performance. This category traces the evolution of theatre from Greek tragedy through Renaissance drama, modern realism, and contemporary experimental playwriting, including the foundational *Natyashastra* of Indian dramaturgy.

Subcategories cover classical drama, Renaissance and Elizabethan drama, modern drama, and contemporary playwriting.
