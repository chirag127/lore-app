Modern drama marks the break from nineteenth-century conventions. Covers Ibsen, Strindberg, Chekhov, Shaw, Brecht, Miller, Williams, Beckett, and the major movements — realism, naturalism, expressionism, epic theatre, and the theatre of the absurd.
