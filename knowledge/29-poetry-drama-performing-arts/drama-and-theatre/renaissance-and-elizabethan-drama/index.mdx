Centered on Shakespeare and his contemporaries (Marlowe, Jonson, Webster), this period represents the high-water mark of English-language drama. Covers the Elizabethan, Jacobean, and Caroline stages, the construction of the Globe, and the cultural context of Renaissance theatre.
