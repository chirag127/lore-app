Poetry compresses meaning into its most potent form. This category spans Western poetry from Homer to modernism, Indian poetry including classical, bhakti, and modern traditions, theory and criticism of poetry, and contemporary spoken word and slam poetry.

## Sub-Subcategories

- **[Indian Poetry](indian-poetry)** — Poetic traditions of the Indian subcontinent from antiquity to the present.
- **[Spoken Word & Slam Poetry](spoken-word-and-slam-poetry)** — Poetry written for performance — oral, rhythmic, and communal.
- **[Theory & Criticism of Poetry](theory-and-criticism-of-poetry)** — How poetry works — prosody, poetics, and the critical tradition.
- **[Western Poetry](western-poetry)** — The epic, lyric, and narrative traditions of Western poetry from Homer to the present.
