Spans the full range of Western poetry: the Homeric epics, Virgil and Ovid, Dante and Petrarch, the English Renaissance (Shakespeare, Donne, Milton), Romanticism (Wordsworth, Keats, Blake), modernism (Eliot, Pound, Yeats, Stevens), and contemporary voices.
