---

Documentary represents reality while shaping it through selection and craft. Covers the documentary tradition from Flaherty and Grierson to direct cinema, cinema verité, essay films, and contemporary streaming-era documentaries. Explores the ethical and epistemological questions unique to non-fiction film.
