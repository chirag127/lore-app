Film theory asks fundamental questions about the nature of cinema: the reality effect, the apparatus, the gaze, spectatorship, and the relationship between film and ideology. Covers major theorists (Bazin, Metz, Mulvey, Deleuze) and the application of critical methods to film analysis.
