*Beethoven: The Universal Composer* is the Pulitzer Prize-winning biographer Edmund Morris's compact and vibrant portrait of the man who changed music forever. Written for the Eminent Lives series, the book distills Beethoven's tumultuous life and revolutionary music into a concise narrative without sacrificing depth or nuance. Morris captures both the sublime artist and the difficult, often repellent human being behind the music.
