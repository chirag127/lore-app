*How Music Works* is David Byrne's idiosyncratic, insightful, and beautifully illustrated exploration of music as a human activity shaped by context. Moving beyond the question of what music is to the question of how it functions in different settings, Byrne draws on his decades of experience as a musician, his observations of musical practices worldwide, and his characteristic intellectual curiosity. The result is a book that challenges musicians and listeners alike to think about music in new ways.
