*Music and the Mind* is the distinguished psychiatrist Anthony Storr's meditation on why music matters so deeply to human beings. Moving nimbly between psychoanalysis, philosophy, and musicology, Storr argues that music is not a mere entertainment but a fundamental human need that gives form and meaning to our emotional lives. Written with the clarity and depth of a mature scholar, the book remains a landmark in the psychological study of music.
