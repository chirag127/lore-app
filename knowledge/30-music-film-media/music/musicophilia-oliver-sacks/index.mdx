<BookHeader />

*Musicophilia* is Oliver Sacks's exploration of the human brain's extraordinary relationship with music, told through his signature medium: the clinical case study. From a man struck by lightning who develops a sudden passion for classical piano to patients with Alzheimer's who can sing songs they can no longer name, Sacks reveals music as a fundamental human need that is wired deep into the neural fabric. The book is both a celebration of music's power and a testament to the resilience of the human brain.
