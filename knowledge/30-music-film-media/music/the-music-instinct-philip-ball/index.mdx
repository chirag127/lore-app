*The Music Instinct* is the most comprehensive single-volume treatment of the science of music available to the general reader. Philip Ball, an acclaimed science writer, tackles the full spectrum of questions about music -- from the physics of sound waves to the neurochemistry of emotion, from the universality of rhythm to the cultural specificity of harmony. The book is more detailed and systematic than Levitin's *This Is Your Brain on Music* and more empirically grounded than Sacks's *Musicophilia*.
