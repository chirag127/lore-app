Susan Weinschenk's *100 Things Every Designer Needs to Know About People* translates cognitive and behavioral psychology into practical design guidance. Each of the 100 short chapters covers one research-backed insight about human behavior, from how people see and read to how they remember, decide, and get motivated.

The book is designed for practitioners who need quick, evidence-based answers to questions like: How many choices should I give users? What makes people trust a website? How do I design for mobile attention spans? Weinschenk, a behavioral psychologist with decades of consulting experience, presents the research in plain language with immediate design implications.
