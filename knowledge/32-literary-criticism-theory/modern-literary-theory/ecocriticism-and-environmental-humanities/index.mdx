title: "Ecocriticism & Environmental Humanities"
parent: "27-literary-criticism/modern-literary-theory"

description: "Literary criticism that examines the relationship between literature and the natural environment."
---

Ecocriticism emerged in the 1990s as a response to the environmental crisis, reading literature through the lens of ecological awareness. Covers foundational texts, the environmental humanities movement, and the intersection of literary study with climate science and environmental ethics.
