description: "Critique of patriarchal structures in literature and the recovery of women's writing."
---

Feminist criticism examines how literature reflects, reinforces, or challenges gender hierarchies. Covers the recovery of neglected women writers, feminist readings of the canon, *écriture féminine*, and the intersection of gender with race, class, and sexuality.
