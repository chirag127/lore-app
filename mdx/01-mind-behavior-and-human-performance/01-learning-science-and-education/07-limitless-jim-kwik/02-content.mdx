## Core Concepts

### The Limitless Model

Kwik's framework has three components: Mindset (your beliefs about learning), Motivation (your why), and Methods (the techniques). All three must be aligned for peak performance. A garbage mindset undermines good methods; strong methods without motivation go unused.

### Speed Reading

Kwik teaches techniques to overcome subvocalization (reading in your inner voice), use peripheral vision, and train eye movement for faster reading. The goal is not just speed but improved comprehension through active engagement with text.

### Memory Techniques

The book covers the major memory systems: the method of loci (memory palace), visualization, association, and chunking. Kwik provides specific protocols for remembering names, numbers, speeches, and lists.

### Digital Brain Fog

Kwik identifies modern information overload as the primary obstacle to cognitive performance. He recommends digital decluttering, mono-tasking, and regular brain breaks to restore focus.

## Practical Applications

- Practice speed reading with a pointer to train eye movement
- Use the memory palace technique for presentations and lists
- Take regular breaks from screens to reduce cognitive fatigue
- Implement a morning brain-training routine
- Use active recall (close the book, summarize) to deepen learning
