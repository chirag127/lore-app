## Narration

### Writing Style

Kwik writes in an energetic, motivational style with short chapters, bullet points, and frequent calls to action. The tone is that of a coach—encouraging, direct, and slightly repetitive for emphasis. The personal narrative of overcoming brain injury is woven throughout.

### Structure

The book is divided into four parts: opening with Kwik's story, then Mindset, Motivation, and Methods. Each chapter ends with practical exercises. The structure is designed for action rather than reflection.

### Rhetorical Techniques

Kwik uses personal transformation narrative, urgent language ("the digital deluge"), and memorable acronyms and frameworks. He positions himself as a guide who overcame the same struggles as readers.

### Readability

Very high. Short chapters, clear headings, and practical exercises make this a quick, actionable read. The tone is accessible to anyone regardless of scientific background.
