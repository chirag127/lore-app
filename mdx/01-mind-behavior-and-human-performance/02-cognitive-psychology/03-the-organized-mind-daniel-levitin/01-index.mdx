Daniel Levitin, a neuroscientist and author of *This Is Your Brain on Music*, applies his deep understanding of cognitive science to the modern problem of information overload. The book explains why the brain struggles with the volume and pace of modern information—and what we can do about it.

Levitin draws on research in attention, memory, categorization, and decision-making to provide a science-based approach to organization. He shows that the brain is not designed for the kind of multitasking, constant interruption, and information volume that characterizes modern life. The solution is not to try harder but to design external systems that work with—rather than against—the brain's natural architecture.
