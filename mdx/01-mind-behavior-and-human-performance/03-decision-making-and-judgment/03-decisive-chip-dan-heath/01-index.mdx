*Decisive* is the Heath brothers' contribution to the decision-making canon — a synthesis of behavioral science research into a practical, memorable framework for making better choices. Building on their trademark style of vivid stories and simple acronyms, they identify four "villains" of decision-making and provide a four-step process — WRAP — to defeat them.



The book's genius is its compression of a vast research literature into an actionable framework. Each of the four villains corresponds to a stage of the decision process, and each has a specific countermeasure. The result is a system that is memorable enough to use in real time yet grounded enough to produce genuinely better outcomes.
