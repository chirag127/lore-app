## Narration

### Writing Style

Guise writes in a direct, conversational style. The tone is encouraging and slightly self-deprecating. The book reads like a friend sharing what worked for him rather than an expert issuing prescriptions.

### Structure

Part personal story, part practical guide. Guise opens with his own struggles and the discovery of the mini habits approach, then provides a step-by-step implementation guide.

### Rhetorical Techniques

Guise uses the "simple solution to a universal problem" structure: everyone struggles with habits, and the solution is almost absurdly simple. The personal narrative makes the approach feel authentic and achievable.

### Readability

Very high. Short chapters, clear language, and practical focus. Can be read in a single sitting. The core idea is simple enough to grasp in minutes.
