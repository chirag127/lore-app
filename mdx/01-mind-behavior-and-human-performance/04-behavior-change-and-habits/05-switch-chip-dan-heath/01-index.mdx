Switch is the definitive book on how to make change happen, whether in your own life, your organization, or your community. Chip and Dan Heath, the authors of Made to Stick, synthesize decades of research in psychology, sociology, and organizational behavior into a memorable framework built around the Rider (analytical mind), the Elephant (emotional mind), and the Path (the environment). The book argues that successful change follows a consistent pattern — direct the Rider, motivate the Elephant, shape the Path — and provides specific techniques for each element.
