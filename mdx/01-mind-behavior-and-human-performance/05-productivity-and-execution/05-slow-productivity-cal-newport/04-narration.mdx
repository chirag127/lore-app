## Narration

### Writing Style

Newport writes with characteristic clarity and conviction. His prose is direct and persuasive, without academic jargon or excessive self-help enthusiasm. The tone is that of a thoughtful critic who has diagnosed a genuine problem.

### Structure

Part one diagnoses the problem of pseudo-productivity. Part two presents the three principles of slow productivity with historical examples. Part three addresses implementation concerns.

### Rhetorical Techniques

Newport uses the "problem and solution" structure effectively, building a compelling case that something is broken before offering a fix. Historical examples of great thinkers provide aspirational models.

### Readability

High. Newport is a clear, efficient writer. The concepts are straightforward and well-illustrated. The book is shorter and more focused than Deep Work.
