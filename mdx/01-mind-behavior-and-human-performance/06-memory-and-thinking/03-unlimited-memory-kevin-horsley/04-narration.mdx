## Writing Style

Horsley writes in a direct, instructional tone reminiscent of a personal coach. His sentences are short and declarative. He uses bold text liberally for emphasis. The style is functional rather than literary — it communicates information efficiently but offers little reading pleasure.

## Structure

The book follows a logical progression: build foundational skills (concentration, visualization), then apply them (link method, PEG system, memory palace). Each chapter is 10-15 pages, making it easy to digest in short sessions.

## Readability

Very accessible. Reading level is approximately 7th grade. Horsley defines terms clearly and avoids all academic jargon.

## Best Use

Read this book as a quick refresher after mastering the techniques from *Remember It!* The exercises are solid, and the compact format makes it easy to revisit specific techniques.
