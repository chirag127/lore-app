## Writing Style

David writes in a clear, compassionate, and highly readable style. She opens with personal stories (including her journey from South Africa and her father's death) that establish emotional credibility. The tone is warm but not sentimental — she is a clinician offering tools, not a guru offering salvation.

## Structure

The book follows the four-step framework, with one section per step. Each chapter within a section explains a concept, illustrates it with research and examples, and ends with practice exercises.

## Readability

Very accessible. Reading level is approximately 9th grade. David avoids jargon and explains psychological concepts clearly.

## Best Use

Read this immediately after *Emotional Intelligence*. Use the four-step framework as a daily practice for handling difficult emotions.
