## Strengths

- **Research-grounded.** Brackett is a leading academic researcher. The RULER system has been tested in hundreds of schools.
- **Extremely practical.** The Mood Meter alone is worth the price of the book.
- **Accessible.** Complex research translated into clear tools.
- **Focus on education.** The school-based research provides unique insight into how emotional skills develop.
- **Honest about limits.** Brackett acknowledges that emotional regulation is hard and that the book is a starting point.

## Weaknesses

- **Education-centric.** The RULER system was designed for schools; the corporate applications feel added on.
- **Light on neuroscience.** Brackett focuses on behavior and outcomes, not brain mechanisms.
- **Repetitive in the middle.** The chapter-by-chapter breakdown of RULER can feel like padding.

| Dimension | Permission to Feel | Emotional Agility |

## Overall

Read this after *Emotional Agility* for the most practical tool-based approach. Brackett's Mood Meter is the single most useful tool in this subcategory for daily emotional awareness.
