## Writing Style

Levine (a psychiatrist) and Heller (a psychologist) write in a clear, accessible style that balances scientific authority with warm, practical advice. The tone is educational but not academic — they explain complex research in everyday language.

## Structure

The book is organized into four sections: the science, the three styles, finding love, and building security. Each chapter combines research summaries, case studies, and practical exercises.

## Readability

Very accessible. Reading level is approximately 9th grade. Psychological terms are clearly defined.

## Best Use

Read this first in the relationship subcategory. Identify your attachment style and your partner's. Use this awareness as the foundation for everything that follows.
