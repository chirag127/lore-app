## Writing Style

Gottman writes in a clear, authoritative, research-grounded style. Nan Silver, his co-writer, keeps the prose accessible. The tone is warm but scientific — Gottman is a researcher first, a therapist second.

## Structure

The book is organized around the seven principles, with one or two chapters per principle. Each chapter opens with a case study, presents the research, and closes with exercises.

## Readability

Very accessible. Reading level is approximately 9th grade. Research findings are presented as simple, memorable insights.

## Best Use

Read this second, after *Attached*. Do the exercises with your partner. The Love Maps and Appreciation exercises can transform a relationship in minutes.
