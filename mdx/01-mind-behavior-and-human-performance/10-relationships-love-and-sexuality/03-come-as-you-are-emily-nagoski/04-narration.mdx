## Writing Style

Nagoski writes with warmth, humor, and exceptional clarity. She has a gift for making complex science feel simple and personal. Her voice is that of a wise, non-judgmental friend who happens to know an enormous amount about sexual science. The book is laced with pop culture references, personal anecdotes, and occasional sass.

## Structure

The book is organized into four parts: the dual control model, desire, arousal and orgasm, and context. Each chapter builds on the previous. Nagoski includes "Try This" exercises throughout.

## Readability

Very accessible. Reading level is approximately 10th grade. Nagoski's conversational style makes even the densest science readable.

## Best Use

Read this third in the relationship subcategory. Use it as a reference for understanding female sexuality and as a tool for conversations with partners.
