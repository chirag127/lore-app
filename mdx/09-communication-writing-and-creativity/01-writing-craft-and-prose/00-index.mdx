# ✍️ Writing Craft & Nonfiction

## 📚 Overview

Writing is thinking made visible. This shelf covers the craft of nonfiction writing: from William Zinsser's classic On Writing Well to Barbara Minto's Pyramid Principle, from narrative nonfiction to technical and scientific writing.

These books will make you a clearer thinker and a more effective communicator.

## ⭐ Essential Books

- **On Writing Well** — William Zinsser: The classic guide to writing nonfiction — clear, concise, and human.
- **The Pyramid Principle** — Barbara Minto: A framework for structured thinking and communication used by McKinsey consultants.
- **Narrative Nonfiction Craft** — How to tell true stories with the tools of fiction.
- **Technical and Scientific Writing** — How to communicate complex ideas clearly and accurately.
