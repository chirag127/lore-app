# 📖 Storytelling

## 📚 Overview

Humans are storytelling animals. We make sense of the world through narratives. This shelf covers both the theory and the practice of storytelling: from story structure and narrative theory to brand storytelling and corporate narrative.

## 🎯 Scope

| Included | Excluded |
|----------|----------|
| Story structure and narrative theory | Writing craft (→ Writing Craft) |
| Brand storytelling and corporate narrative | Public speaking (→ Public Speaking) |
| The science of why stories work | Fiction (→ Fiction & Literature) |

## 🔗 Category Boundaries

- **Writing Craft** covers the practice of writing.
- **Public Speaking** covers oral storytelling.
- **Fiction & Literature** covers fictional narratives.

This category focuses on the **theory and strategy** of storytelling.

## 📖 Topics Covered

- Story structure: the anatomy of compelling narratives
- Narrative theory: why stories work
- Brand storytelling: using narrative to build brands
- Corporate narrative: storytelling in business

Stories are the most powerful communication tool humans have. Understanding how they work helps you communicate more effectively, build stronger brands, and connect more deeply with your audience.
