# 🎤 Persuasion & Rhetoric

## 📚 Overview

Persuasion is the art of changing minds. This shelf covers both the classical tradition — Aristotle's rhetoric, the art of argumentation — and modern applied persuasion techniques.

Whether you're pitching a product, making a speech, or negotiating a deal, these books provide the tools for effective persuasion.
