# 🎙️ Public Speaking & Presentation

## 📚 Overview

Public speaking is the art of communicating ideas to an audience. This shelf covers both the theory and the practice: from rhetorical theory and presentation design to visual communication and the craft of delivering a compelling talk.

## 🔗 Category Boundaries

- **Persuasion & Rhetoric** covers the art of persuasion.
- **Storytelling** covers narrative techniques.

This category focuses on **speaking to audiences** — the skills and techniques of effective public communication.
