# 🔤 Linguistics

## 📚 Overview

Linguistics is the scientific study of language: its structure, meaning, use, and variation. This shelf covers the major branches: phonetics and phonology, morphology and syntax, semantics and pragmatics, cognitive linguistics and metaphor theory, historical and comparative linguistics, and sociolinguistics.

These books provide the scientific foundation for understanding how human language works.

- Phonetics and phonology: the sounds of language

## ⭐ Essential Books

- **Phonetics and Phonology** — The science of speech sounds and how they pattern.
- **Morphology and Syntax** — The structure of words and sentences across languages.
- **Semantics and Pragmatics** — How meaning is constructed and communicated.
- **Cognitive Linguistics and Metaphor Theory** — How language reflects and shapes thought.
- **Sociolinguistics and Language Variation** — How language varies across social groups and contexts.
