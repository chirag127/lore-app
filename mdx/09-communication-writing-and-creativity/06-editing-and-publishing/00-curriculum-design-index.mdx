# 📐 Curriculum Design & Instructional Design

## 📚 Overview

Good instruction doesn't happen by accident. This shelf covers the science of designing effective learning experiences: curriculum theory, instructional design models, learning objectives, and assessment strategies.

These books are essential for anyone who designs training, teaches, or creates educational content.

## 🔗 Category Boundaries

- **Learning Science** covers the science of how people learn.
- **Language Learning** covers language acquisition.
- **Philosophy & History of Education** covers educational philosophy.

This category focuses on the **design of instruction** — how to structure learning experiences for maximum impact.

- Curriculum theory: what to teach and why
- Instructional design models: ADDIE, SAM, and beyond
- Learning objectives and assessment: how to measure learning
