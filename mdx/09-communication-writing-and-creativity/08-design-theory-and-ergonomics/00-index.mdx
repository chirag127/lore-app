# Design Theory & Ergonomics

## Overview

Good design is invisible. This shelf covers the essential books on design theory and usability: Norman's foundational human-centered design, Krug's practical web usability, and Lidwell's reference of universal design principles.

## Scope

| Included | Excluded |
|----------|----------|
| Human-centered design theory | Graphic design technique (→ Visual Arts) |
| Web usability | Branding and identity design |
| Cross-disciplinary design principles | Fashion and textile design |
| Usability testing | Game design (→ Entertainment) |

## Essential Books

- **The Design of Everyday Things** — Don Norman: The foundational text of human-centered design.
- **Don't Make Me Think** — Steve Krug: The most accessible introduction to web usability.
- **Universal Principles of Design** — Lidwell, Holden & Butler: A comprehensive reference of 125 design principles.
