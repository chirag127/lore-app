# Architecture & Spatial Design

## Overview

Architecture shapes our experience of the world at every scale, from a single room to the city. This shelf covers three essential perspectives: Ching's systematic visual grammar of architectural elements, de Botton's philosophical inquiry into why buildings affect our well-being, and Venturi's postmodern manifesto that challenged modernist orthodoxy.

## Essential Books

- **Architecture: Form, Space, and Order** — Francis D.K. Ching: The visual grammar of architectural design.
- **The Architecture of Happiness** — Alain de Botton: A philosophical exploration of why buildings affect our well-being.
- **Complexity and Contradiction in Architecture** — Robert Venturi: The manifesto that launched postmodern architecture.
