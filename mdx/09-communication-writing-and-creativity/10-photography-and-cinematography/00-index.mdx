# Photography & Cinematography

## Overview

The image — still or moving — is the most powerful communication tool of our time. This shelf covers three essential perspectives: Sontag's philosophical critique of photography's social effects, Freeman's systematic guide to photographic composition, and Murch's meditation on the art of film editing.

## Essential Books

- **On Photography** — Susan Sontag: A landmark philosophical critique of photography.
- **The Photographer's Eye** — Michael Freeman: The definitive guide to photographic composition.
- **In the Blink of an Eye** — Walter Murch: A master editor's philosophy of film editing.
