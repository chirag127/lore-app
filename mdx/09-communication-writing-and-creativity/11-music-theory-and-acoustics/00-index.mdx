# Music Theory & Acoustics

## Overview

Music may be the most universal human art. This shelf covers three essential perspectives: David Byrne's cultural and technological history of how music is shaped by its context, Oliver Sacks's neurological case studies of music's power over the brain, and Daniel Levitin's systematic explanation of the neuroscience of musical experience.

## Essential Books

- **How Music Works** — David Byrne: A wide-ranging exploration of how context, technology, and economics shape music.
- **Musicophilia** — Oliver Sacks: Tales of music and the brain from the master of the neurological case study.
- **This Is Your Brain on Music** — Daniel Levitin: The definitive popular science account of music neuroscience.
