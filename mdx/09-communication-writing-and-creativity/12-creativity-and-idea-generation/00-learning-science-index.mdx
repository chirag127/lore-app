# 🧠 Learning Science & Cognitive Science of Learning

## 📚 Overview

How do people actually learn? This shelf covers the cognitive science of learning: how memory works, how expertise develops, how spaced repetition and retrieval practice strengthen retention, and how AI is transforming education.

These books bridge the gap between cognitive science research and practical educational application.
