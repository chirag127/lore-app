# Strengths

## Strength 1: Comprehensive Coverage

Broughton covers everything a new editor needs to know, from basic markup to advanced templates to dispute resolution. The book is genuinely comprehensive within its scope.

## Strength 2: Practical, Hands-On Approach

The Missing Manual series format emphasizes practical guidance. Broughton provides step-by-step instructions, screenshots, and concrete examples that readers can follow. This makes the book immediately useful rather than merely informative.

## Strength 3: Policy Expertise

Broughton's understanding of Wikipedia's policies and community norms is deep and nuanced. He explains not just what the policies say but how they are actually applied in practice, which is essential knowledge for effective editing.

# Weaknesses

## Weakness 1: Technical Datedness

Published in 2008, the book is now significantly out of date in technical details. Wikipedia's interface has changed dramatically with Visual Editor and mobile editing, and many specific procedures are different. The social and policy guidance remains relevant, but the technical sections require supplementation.

## Weakness 2: Lack of Coverage of Advanced Tools

The book predates or gives minimal coverage to tools that have become essential for serious editors: AutoWikiBrowser, Huggle, Twinkle, and other semi-automated editing tools. A modern guide would need to cover these extensively.

## Weakness 3: Focus on Desktop Editing

The book assumes desktop-based editing and does not cover the mobile editing experience, which has become increasingly important as mobile users account for a growing share of Wikipedia traffic and edits.

# Criticism

## Critic 1: *O'Reilly Media*

"Typical of the Missing Manual series, this book is thorough, well-organized, and practical. Broughton writes with authority and clarity. The book fills a genuine need for a comprehensive Wikipedia editing guide."

## Critic 2: *Wikipedia Signpost* (community newsletter)

"A valuable resource for new editors, though experienced Wikipedians may find some sections basic. The policy explanations are generally accurate and well-considered. The book shows its age in technical details but remains the best guide of its kind."

# Historical Context

## Publication Context

Published in 2008, at a time when Wikipedia was transitioning from its early free-for-all era to a more structured, policy-governed project. The book captured Wikipedia at a pivotal moment when the community was professionalizing its editorial processes while maintaining its open-editing ethos.

## Legacy and Influence

The book has been cited in Wikipedia's own documentation and is frequently recommended to new editors. It established a standard for Wikipedia how-to guides that has not been surpassed, though the technical obsolescence means a second edition would be valuable.

# Final Assessment

**8/10** — The definitive practical guide to Wikipedia editing, comprehensive and well-structured. The technical datedness is a material limitation, but the social and policy guidance remains invaluable. A second edition would be most welcome.
