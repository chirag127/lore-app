# The World Almanac and Book of Facts 2025

## Executive Summary

The World Almanac is America's best-selling annual reference work, providing a comprehensive snapshot of the world. The 2025 edition covers major events of 2024, updated statistics on every country and US state, and reference data on topics from astronomy to zoology. For over 150 years, the World Almanac has been the most trusted source for facts and figures about the world.



## Key Takeaways

- **Most comprehensive annual reference** in the United States
- **Covers** politics, economics, science, technology, sports, and culture
- **Includes historical data** for year-over-year comparison
- **Special sections** on major events of the past year
- **Reference data** on all 50 states and countries worldwide
