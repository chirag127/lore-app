# Strengths

## Strength 1: Authoritative Data

The World Almanac is a trusted source with rigorous fact-checking. Data is sourced from government agencies, international organizations, and other authoritative bodies.

## Strength 2: Comprehensive Scope

Virtually any factual question about the world can be answered with the Almanac. Its breadth is unmatched among single-volume reference works.

## Strength 3: Consistent Categories

Year-over-year consistency allows readers to track changes across editions, making the Almanac a tool for understanding trends.

# Weaknesses

## Weakness 1: US Focus

Despite the title "World Almanac," the coverage is heavily weighted toward the United States. International data is included but is less detailed than US coverage.

## Weakness 2: Digital Competition

For many users, online databases provide more current data and easier searchability. The print Almanac's value lies in its curated, authoritative content.

## Weakness 3: Bulk and Weight

At over 1,000 pages, the print edition is physically large. It is a desk reference, not something you carry.

# Criticism

## Critic 1: *Library Journal*

"An essential reference work for libraries of all sizes. The World Almanac provides reliable, up-to-date information on virtually any topic. The 2025 edition maintains the high standards of its predecessors."

## Critic 2: *Booklist*

"The World Almanac remains the gold standard for annual reference works. Comprehensive, reliable, and well-organized. Essential for any reference collection."

# Historical Context

## 150 Years of the Almanac

The World Almanac was first published in 1868. For over 150 years, it has adapted to changing times while maintaining its core mission: providing reliable facts about the world to the American public.

## The Almanac Tradition

The World Almanac is part of a long tradition of almanacs dating back centuries. Almanacs were once essential sources of astronomical, agricultural, and astrological information. The World Almanac modernized the format for an industrial and information age.

# Final Assessment

**9/10** — The most comprehensive and reliable annual reference work available. Its scope, authority, and consistent organization make it an essential reference for any home, school, or office. The US focus is a limitation, but within its scope it is unmatched.
